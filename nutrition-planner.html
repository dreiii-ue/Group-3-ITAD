Fuel for Thought

Optimizing Your Macros

What you eat directly impacts your cognitive function. For those balancing long study sessions, a balance of complex carbohydrates and lean proteins is essential.

By planning your meals, you avoid the energy crashes caused by processed sugars and maintain steady focus throughout the day.
