Strava

What is Strava?

The World's Leading Fitness Community App

Strava is a fitness tracking platform designed for athletes of all kinds. It connects you with a global community to analyze performance and stay consistent. Whether you're running, cycling, or walking, Strava turns every effort into a social experience that helps you reach your fitness goals. With powerful GPS tracking, personalized insights, and a vibrant community of millions worldwide, Strava transforms ordinary workouts into meaningful achievements.

Benefits

Why Students Use Strava

Strava isn't just a tracker, it's a motivational tool that makes fitness a social, data-driven habit.

Real-Time Performance Data

Get instant stats on pace, distance, and calories so you know how hard you're working.

Social Accountability

Seeing your friends' activities keeps you inspired. Kudos create a positive campus culture.

Goal Setting & Challenges

Monthly distance challenges help you set goals and build consistent exercise habits.

Training History

Your activity log makes it easy to spot trends and celebrate your long-term progress.

Safe Route Awareness

Strava heatmaps help you discover safe, popular paths around campus and the city.

Wearable Integration

Connects seamlessly with Apple Watch, Garmin, and Fitbit to sync all your health data.
