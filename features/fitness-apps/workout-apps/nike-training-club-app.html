Nike Training Club

What is Nike Training Club?

A Free All-in-One Fitness App by Nike

Nike Training Club (NTC) is a free fitness app by Nike that gives you access to over 185 trainer-led and whiteboard workouts covering strength, HIIT, yoga, pilates, core, and recovery — all without needing a gym or equipment. With on-demand classes led by world-class Nike trainers, personalized goal-setting tools, multi-week training programs, and wellness tips on nutrition, sleep, and mindfulness, NTC is designed to help every athlete — from beginner to advanced — train smarter and stay consistent.

How To Use

Start Training with Nike Training Club in 6 Simple Steps

1

Download & Create a Nike Account

Install the NTC app and sign in with your Nike Member account — it's free. Answer a few questions about your goals so the app can suggest where to start.

2

Browse & Filter Workouts

Explore the workout library and filter by type (strength, HIIT, yoga, recovery), duration, difficulty level, or equipment — including a no-equipment filter for full home workouts.

3

Follow a Training Program

Join a structured 4–6 week training plan designed for specific goals like building strength, getting leaner, or improving endurance — complete with weekly workout schedules.

4

Work Out with Trainer-Led Videos

Follow along with on-demand video classes led by Nike trainers. Each session includes warm-up, main workout, and cooldown with full audio coaching and technique tips.

5

Schedule & Cast Workouts

Plan sessions in advance with the workout scheduling feature, and cast workouts to your TV for a bigger screen experience right from your living room.

6

Track Activity & Log Progress

Log all your workouts in the Activity tab and sync with Apple Health or Google Fit to keep a complete record of your training journey and stay motivated.
