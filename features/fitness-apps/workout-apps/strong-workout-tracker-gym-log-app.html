What is Strong?

The Simplest Workout Tracker for Serious Lifters

Strong – Workout Tracker Gym Log is a highly-rated fitness app by Strong Fitness PTE. LTD. that lets you record every workout quickly and effortlessly. Used by over 3 million lifters worldwide, Strong is designed around speed and simplicity, log your sets, reps, and weights in seconds, track personal records, monitor progression through detailed charts, and build or customize your own workout routines, all without the clutter of unnecessary features.

How To Use

Start Logging with Strong in 6 Simple Steps

1

Create an Account

Sign up and set up your profile to start logging workouts and syncing your data across devices with Cloud Sync.

2

Set Up a Routine or Template

Build your own workout routine by choosing from a comprehensive library of strength and cardio exercises, or create custom ones that fit your training style.

3

Start a Workout & Log Your Sets

Begin your session and log each exercise's sets, reps, and weight with just a few taps. Tag sets as warm-up, failure, or drop sets as needed.

4

Use the Rest Timer & Plate Calculator

Let the built-in auto countdown timer track your rest breaks, and use the Plate Calculator and Warm-Up Calculator to plan your weights precisely.

5

Review Progress with Charts

After your session, analyze your volume and one-rep max progression through detailed graphs to see exactly how your strength is improving over time.

6

Save as Template & Share

Save great workouts as reusable templates for future sessions, and share your routines or workout summaries with friends directly from the app.
