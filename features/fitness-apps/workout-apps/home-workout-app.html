What is Home Workout – No Equipment?

A Complete Bodyweight Fitness App for Everyone

Home Workout – No Equipment is a free fitness app developed by Leap Fitness Group that provides expert-designed daily workout routines targeting every major muscle group, abs, chest, legs, arms, and full body, without requiring any gym equipment or a coach. Using only your bodyweight, animations, and video guidance, the app helps you build strength, burn calories, and track your fitness progress right from home.

How To Use

Start Working Out with Home Workout in 6 Simple Steps

1

Download & Set Up Your Profile

Install the app and enter your details such as age, weight, and fitness level so it can tailor the right workout difficulty for you.

2

Choose Your Target Muscle Group

Select which part of your body you want to train, abs, chest, arms, legs, butt, or full body, and the app will load the appropriate workout plan.

3

Follow the Warm-Up Routine

Begin each session with the built-in warm-up and stretching routines to prepare your body and reduce the risk of injury before training.

4

Exercise with Animation & Video Guidance

Perform each exercise by following detailed animations and instructional videos that show proper form and technique for every movement.

5

Track Your Progress

Let the app automatically record your workout history, weight trends, and calories burned so you can monitor your improvement over time.

6

Set Reminders & Stay Consistent

Customize workout reminders to keep you on schedule, and share your achievements on social media to celebrate your fitness milestones.
