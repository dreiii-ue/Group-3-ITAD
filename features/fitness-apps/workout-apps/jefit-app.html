What is JEFIT?

The Ultimate Gym Workout Planner & Tracker

JEFIT is a comprehensive gym workout planner and tracker used by over 13 million fitness enthusiasts worldwide. It gives you access to 1,300+ exercises with HD videos, 2,500+ workout routines, and powerful tools to log sets, reps, weights, and rest periods during every session. With detailed progress charts, 1RM tracking, muscle recovery breakdown, a built-in community, and smartwatch support, JEFIT is built for gym-goers who want to train smarter and see measurable results.

How To Use

Start Training with JEFIT in 6 Simple Steps

1

Create Your Account

Sign up for a free JEFIT account on the app or website. Set up your profile with your fitness level, goals, and body measurements to personalize your experience.

2

Choose or Build a Workout Routine

Browse thousands of expert-designed routines by fitness level, muscle group, or goal, or use the intuitive workout builder to create a fully customized plan with your preferred exercises, sets, rest times, and supersets.

3

Learn Exercises with HD Videos

Before and during your workout, review detailed HD video instructions and exercise descriptions for any of the 1,300+ exercises in the JEFIT library to ensure correct form.

4

Log Sets, Reps & Weights

During your session, log each exercise's sets, reps, and weights with a few taps. Use the built-in rest timer between sets and add personal notes for future reference.

5

Analyze Progress with Charts

After your workout, review dynamic progress graphs tracking volume, 1RM improvements, workout duration, and muscle recovery breakdown to understand exactly how your fitness is developing.

6

Engage with the Community

Share your workout milestones and personal bests, join community strength challenges, and connect with other gym-goers and trainers to stay motivated and accountable.
