What is Peloton Yoga?

Instructor-Led, Community-Driven Fitness

Peloton Yoga is a premium digital fitness experience that offers thousands of live and on-demand classes. Rooted in five key elements—Foundation, Power, Focus, Recovery, and Unity—Peloton combines top-tier athletic instruction with immersive music. Whether you're looking for a rigorous Power Flow or a desk-friendly "Yoga Anywhere" session, Peloton connects you with world-class instructors and a global leaderboard to keep you motivated.

How To Use

Find Your Flow in 6 Simple Steps

1

Open the App & Filter

Navigate to the Yoga category and use the filters to narrow down classes by instructor, music genre, or class length (from 5 to 90 minutes).

2

Choose Your Style

Select from Flow (Vinyasa), Slow Flow, Restorative, or Power Yoga based on whether you want to build heat or wind down for the day.

3

Explore Specialized "Focus Flows"

Target specific areas like hips, core, or back, or dive deep into "Yoga Basics" to master specific poses and improve your form.

4

Check Your Equipment

Review the class description to see if you'll need props like blocks, straps, or a bolster to support your practice and enhance your range of motion.

5

Join a Live or On-Demand Class

Jump into a live session to practice with others in real-time or hit play on an on-demand class that fits perfectly into your morning schedule.

6

Track Your Progress

Earn badges, hit milestones, and view your workout history in the Profile tab to stay consistent and celebrate your wellness journey.
