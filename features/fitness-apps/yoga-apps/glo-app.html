Glo

What is Glo?

A Holistic Digital Wellness Studio

Glo is an online yoga, meditation, Pilates, and fitness platform featuring over 5,000 classes. It focuses on the "why" behind movement, using smart technology to suggest classes based on your current mood and physical needs, ensuring a deeply personal practice led by world-class instructors.

Editor's Rating: 4.7 / 5

How To Use

Personalize Your Practice in 6 Simple Steps

1

Tell Glo About Yourself

Complete onboarding by selecting your style, experience level, and goals like better sleep or flexibility.

2

Discover Your Feed

Check the "For You" tab daily for curated recommendations that align with your profile and activity history.

3

Filter for Your Needs

Find classes by duration (5 to 90+ mins), intensity, or search for your favorite specific teacher.

4

Join a Program

Enroll in structured series designed for long-term results, like "Beginner Yoga" or "Core Strength Clinic."

5

Schedule Your Practice

Add classes to your Glo Calendar or sync with your phone to ensure you prioritize your well-being.

6

Save and Download

Build a library of favorites and download them for offline use so you can practice anywhere, anytime.

Evaluation

Pros & Cons

Pros

  • One of the largest libraries (5,000+) available.
  • Excellent search functionality and specific filtering.
  • Highly reputable instructors with diverse teaching backgrounds.
  • Focuses on both mental education and physical fitness.

Cons

  • Subscription model can be pricey for casual users.
  • The library size can be overwhelming for new members.
  • Minimal social interaction features compared to live-stream apps.
  • Some older content lacks the high production of newer videos.

Verdict

Final Review

Glo is the ultimate choice for practitioners who want variety and depth. It is more than just a fitness app; it is a learning platform. If you want to dive deep into the philosophy of yoga or need a structured path to Pilates mastery, Glo's library and scheduling tools make it a premier choice for long-term health.
