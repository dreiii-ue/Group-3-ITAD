Glo

What is Glo?

A Holistic Digital Wellness Studio

Glo is an online yoga, meditation, Pilates, and fitness platform that brings together world-class instructors to guide you through a deeply personal practice. With over 5,000 classes ranging from beginner to advanced, Glo focuses on the "why" behind the movement. The platform uses smart technology to suggest classes based on your current mood, physical needs, and time constraints, ensuring that your wellness journey is as unique as you are.

How To Use

Personalize Your Practice in 6 Simple Steps

1

Tell Glo About Yourself

Complete your onboarding by selecting your favorite styles, experience level, and the specific goals you want to achieve, like better sleep or increased flexibility.

2

Discover Your Daily Feed

Check your "For You" tab every morning for curated class recommendations that align with your profile and previous activity.

3

Filter for Your Needs

Use the search filters to find exactly what you need right now—search by duration (as short as 5 mins), intensity, or even a specific teacher you love.

4

Join a Program

Enroll in a "Program"—a structured series of classes—designed to help you achieve long-term results, such as "Beginner Yoga" or "Core Strength Clinic."

5

Schedule Your Practice

Add classes directly to your personal "Glo Calendar" or sync them with your phone’s calendar to ensure you make time for your well-being.

6

Save and Download

Keep a library of your favorite classes and download them for offline use so you can practice even without an internet connection.
