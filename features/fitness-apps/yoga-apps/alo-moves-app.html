What is Alo Moves?

A Premium Yoga and Wellness Platform

Alo Moves is the official digital platform of Alo Yoga, offering thousands of classes in yoga, fitness, and meditation. Whether you're a beginner or an advanced yogi, the platform provides beautifully filmed, expert-led series designed to help you master new skills and find mental clarity.

Editor's Rating: 4.9 / 5

How To Use

Start Your Practice in 6 Simple Steps

1

Set Up Your Profile

Create an account and specify your fitness goals and experience level for tailored recommendations.

2

Browse by Category

Filter classes by style (Vinyasa, Pilates, HIIT), difficulty level, instructor, or time.

3

Commit to a Series

Join progressive, multi-day series to master specific poses or build foundational strength.

4

Download for Offline

Save your favorite flows directly to your device to practice anywhere without an internet connection.

5

Explore Mindfulness

Engage in guided meditations, breathwork, and sound baths to balance your physical workouts.

6

Track Consistency

Monitor your completed classes and build activity streaks to stay motivated on your journey.

Evaluation

Pros & Cons

Pros

  • The highest quality video production in the fitness app market.
  • Expert skill-based series (e.g., Learning to Handstand).
  • Clean, aesthetic, and intuitive user interface.
  • Daily new content releases to keep practices fresh.

Cons

  • Higher monthly subscription cost than competitors.
  • No live-streaming classes; content is entirely pre-recorded.
  • Can be overwhelming for total beginners due to the vast library.
  • Requires a stable high-speed connection for 4K streaming.

Verdict

Final Review

Alo Moves is the gold standard for at-home yoga. While it comes at a premium price, the sheer quality of instruction and production makes it feel like having a luxury studio in your living room. It is best suited for those who are serious about advancing their yoga skills or those who value the aesthetic and mindful aspects of their fitness routine.
