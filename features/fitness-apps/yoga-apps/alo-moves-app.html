What is Alo Moves?

A Premium Yoga and Wellness Platform

Alo Moves is the official app of Alo Yoga, offering thousands of classes in yoga, fitness, and meditation. Whether you're a beginner looking to build a foundation or an advanced yogi mastering new skills, the platform provides beautifully filmed, expert-led series that you can practice anywhere. It focuses on holistic wellness, combining physical movement with mental clarity.

How To Use

Start Your Practice with Alo Moves in 6 Simple Steps

1

Set Up Your Profile

Create an account and complete a brief questionnaire about your fitness goals, preferred class styles, and experience level to receive personalized recommendations.

2

Browse by Category

Explore the expansive library by filtering classes by style (Vinyasa, Ashtanga, Pilates, HIIT), difficulty level, specific instructor, or duration.

3

Commit to a Series

Join a progressive, multi-day class series designed to help you master specific poses, build foundational strength, or cultivate a daily wellness habit.

4

Download for Offline Use

Save your favorite flows and meditations directly to your device so you can maintain your practice anywhere, even without an internet connection.

5

Explore Mindfulness

Balance your physical workouts by engaging in the app’s extensive collection of guided meditations, breathwork exercises, and relaxing sound baths.

6

Track Your Consistency

Log your completed classes automatically, monitor your weekly activity, and build streaks to stay motivated and committed to your wellness journey.
