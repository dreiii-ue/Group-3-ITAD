Yoga With Adriene

What is Yoga With Adriene?

The World's Largest Online Yoga Community

Yoga With Adriene (YWA) is a mission-driven community led by Adriene Mishler. With a focus on making yoga accessible to all bodies and budgets, YWA provides high-quality yoga and mindfulness practices via YouTube and the "Find What Feels Good" (FWFG) platform. Known for her down-to-earth style and her dog, Benji, Adriene helps practitioners of all levels find mental and physical relief while building a deep, personal connection to their practice.

How To Use

Start Your Journey in 6 Simple Steps

1

Visit the YouTube Channel

Access the library of over 600 free yoga videos on the Yoga With Adriene YouTube channel, searchable by need, time, or mood.

2

Pick Your Practice

Search for specific themes like "Yoga for Back Pain," "Yoga for Anxiety," or "Yoga for Beginners" to find a class tailored to your current state.

3

Join a 30-Day Journey

Every January, Adriene hosts a 30-day yoga series. You can start these themed journeys anytime to build a consistent, daily habit.

4

Focus on Your Breath

Listen to Adriene's cues on "Pranayama" (breathwork). The practice emphasizes moving with intention and listening to your body's limits.

5

Engage with the Community

Read comments and join the global conversation. The YWA community is famous for being supportive, kind, and welcoming to newcomers.

6

Explore FWFG Kula

For a deeper experience, explore the "Find What Feels Good" membership app for exclusive content, offline viewing, and community forums.

The YWA Philosophy

Self-Compassion

Adriene encourages you to "find what feels good" rather than forcing poses, fostering a kind and sustainable relationship with exercise.

Zero Cost Entry

With hundreds of free videos on YouTube, high-quality yoga instruction is available to anyone with an internet connection, regardless of income.

Playful Spirit

The sessions often include humor, guest appearances by Benji the dog, and a relaxed atmosphere that removes the intimidation factor of yoga.
