What is Down Dog?

A Highly Customizable Yoga Generator

Down Dog is a unique fitness app that dynamically generates a new yoga practice every time you use it. Unlike pre-recorded videos, Down Dog builds a custom flow based on your chosen level, duration, focus area, and music. With over 60,000 different configurations, it ensures you never have to do the exact same workout twice, keeping your practice fresh and engaging.

How To Use

Generate Your Perfect Flow in 6 Simple Steps

1

Select Your Practice Type

Choose from a variety of yoga styles including Vinyasa, Hatha, Restorative, Yin, Ashtanga, or Chair yoga based on what your body needs today.

2

Set Your Level & Duration

Pick your experience level (from Beginner to Advanced) and determine how much time you have, whether it's a quick 10-minute stretch or a full 90-minute session.

3

Customize Your Focus

Want to open up your hips or strengthen your core? Select a specific "Boost" area to focus the generated practice on a particular body part or skill.

4

Pick Your Music & Voice

Choose your preferred background music genre (which dynamically rises and falls with your breathing) and select the instructor's voice and pacing.

5

Start Your Unique Flow

Press play and follow along with the high-definition video and clear audio guidance that Down Dog stitches together just for you in real-time.

6

Save & Track Favorites

If you loved the flow you just did, save it to your favorites to practice it again later, and let the app track your overall wellness consistency.
