What is Runna?

Your Personal Running Coach

Runna is the world’s leading personalized running platform. It provides tailored training plans for 5ks, marathons, and everything in between, empowering people to run stronger and injury-free with scientific workout structures and real-time coaching.

How To Use

Start Your Journey in 6 Simple Steps

1

Choose Your Plan

Select a goal, from "couch to 5k" to a "sub-3-hour marathon" tailored to your fitness level.

2

Sync Your Devices

Connect your Garmin, Apple Watch, or COROS to get live pacing during your runs.

3

Strength & Mobility

Follow guided strength workouts designed specifically to support a runner’s body.

4

Dynamic Pacing

Receive real-time speed adjustments based on your progress and recent performance.

5

Track Your Progress

Monitor your weekly mileage, intensity levels, and estimated race finish times.

6

Join the Community

Connect with thousands of Runna athletes and share your PB (Personal Best) milestones.
