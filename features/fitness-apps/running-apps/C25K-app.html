Couch to 5K

What is Couch to 5K?

Your Digital Path to Running

Couch to 5K is designed for people who want to start running but don't know where to begin. It takes you from the sofa to running a full 5K in just nine weeks by using a simple mix of walking and running. The app tells you exactly when to speed up and when to slow down, making progress feel easy rather than overwhelming. It is perfect for anyone who wants to stop guessing and start training with a real plan.

How To Use

Go From Walking to Running in 6 Steps

1

Pick a Trainer

Choose a motivating voice to guide you through your workouts and tell you when to run.

2

Start Small

Begin with short 60-second runs mixed with walking intervals to build your confidence.

3

Follow the Schedule

Complete three workouts a week, leaving rest days in between to let your body recover.

4

Listen for Cues

The app works in the background, chiming in when it's time to switch pace while you listen to music.

5

Track Your Wins

Watch your running intervals get longer each week as your fitness levels naturally improve.

6

Finish Your 5K

By week nine, you will be ready to run for 30 minutes straight without stopping for a break.
