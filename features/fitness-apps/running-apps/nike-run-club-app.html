Nike Run Club

What is Nike Run Club?

A Coaching App for Everyday Runners

Nike Run Club is a running app designed to help users track workouts, follow guided runs, and stay motivated through training plans and challenges. It blends coaching, run data, and community features into one simple platform for runners of different experience levels.

Editor's Rating: 4.8 / 5

App Capabilities

Core Features

Guided Runs

Train with coach-led audio sessions that support both beginners and experienced runners.

Training Plans

Follow structured plans for 5K, 10K, and longer distance goals.

Run Tracking

Log pace, distance, splits, and route details from your phone or watch.

Challenges & Motivation

Stay consistent with badges, milestones, and community-based encouragement.

How To Use

Start Running in 6 Simple Steps

1

Set Your Running Goal

Choose a goal like building consistency, improving pace, or preparing for a race.

2

Choose a Guided Run

Pick an audio-guided run, training plan, or challenge that matches your current level.

3

Pair Your Device

Connect your phone or watch so your distance, pace, and route are tracked accurately.

4

Start Your Run

Begin your workout and listen to coaching cues, motivation, and pacing reminders along the way.

5

Review Your Progress

Check your splits, distance, pace, and run history to see how you are improving.

6

Join the Inner Circle

Attend exclusive member-only live sessions and digital workshops with Nike athletes.
