Garmin Connect

What is Garmin Connect?

Your Personalized Health Dashboard

Think of Garmin Connect as the mission control for your body. It is a data hub that takes information from your watch to show you exactly how you are doing. Instead of just counting steps, it looks at things like your "Body Battery" to tell you if you have enough energy for a hard workout or if you need to take a rest day. It is perfect for anyone who wants to stop guessing and start training with a real plan.

How To Use

Master Your Metrics in 6 Simple Steps

1

Pair Your Device

Sync your Garmin watch via Bluetooth to automatically upload your activities and health data.

2

Customize Your Dashboard

Arrange "My Day" cards to prioritize the stats you care about, like steps, heart rate, or stress.

4

Review detailed maps and charts for every run, including cadence, elevation, and power zones.

Monitor Recovery

Check your Body Battery™ and Sleep Score to determine if you’re ready for a hard session or a rest day.

Earn Badges & Connect

Participate in monthly challenges, earn virtual badges, and compete with friends in the community.
