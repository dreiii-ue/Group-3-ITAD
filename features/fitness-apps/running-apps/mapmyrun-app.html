What is MapMyRun?

Your Ultimate Route Companion

MapMyRun has recently returned to its roots. After years under Under Armour, the original founders bought the app back and moved it over to the Outside Interactive family. It’s still the same reliable tool for tracking your pace and distance, but now it’s plugged into a massive network of outdoor and adventure experts. Whether you’re just trying to get around the block or training for a PR, you get access to millions of community routes and a deeper focus on the overall athlete experience.

The 6-Step Journey

Map Your Success in 6 Simple Steps

1

Create Your Profile

Set your fitness goals and input your physical metrics for more accurate calorie and effort tracking.

2

Find Your Route

Browse millions of user-verified routes or create your own custom path using the map builder.

3

Pair Your Gear

Seamlessly connect UA Connected Footwear or your favorite wearable for Form Coaching and live stats.

4

Start Your Activity

Hit "Start Workout" to get real-time audio feedback on your pace, distance, and duration.

5

Analyze Performance

Deep dive into post-run data to identify trends in your cadence, stride length, and elevation gain.

6

Share & Compete

Join community challenges, climb the leaderboards, and share your achievements with friends.
