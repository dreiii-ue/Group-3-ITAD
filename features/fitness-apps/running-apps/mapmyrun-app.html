What is MapMyRun?

Your Ultimate Route Companion

MapMyRun has recently returned to its roots. After years under Under Armour, the original founders bought the app back and moved it over to the Outside Interactive family. It’s still the same reliable tool for tracking your pace and distance, but now it’s plugged into a massive network of outdoor and adventure experts. Whether you’re just trying to get around the block or training for a PR, you get access to millions of community routes.
