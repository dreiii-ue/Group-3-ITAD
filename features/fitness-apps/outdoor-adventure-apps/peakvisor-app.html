What is PeakVisor?

A Superpower for Mountain Explorers

PeakVisor is an outdoor navigation app that turns your phone into a high-tech mountain guide. By combining your camera with a massive global terrain database, it identifies over a million peaks instantly, provides 3D maps, and tracks your hiking progress with precision.

How To Use

Identify Your Surroundings in 6 Steps

1

Point Your Camera

Aim your device at any mountain range to see names and altitudes instantly.

2

Identify from Photos

Upload past hiking photos to tag peaks you didn't know the names of.

3

Explore in 3D

Use the 3D terrain viewer to visualize your trail before you start walking.

4

Track Peaks Bagged

Keep a digital diary of every summit you reach with the "Peak Bagging" list.

5

Find the Sun

Track the sun's path to find the perfect lighting for your mountain photography.

6

Offline Scouting

Download mountain regions to identify peaks even without an internet connection.
