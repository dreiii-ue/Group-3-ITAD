What is Outdooractive?

The Digital Home of Outdoor Sports

Outdooractive connects a global community of hikers, cyclists, and skiers with official content from tourism partners. It offers a sophisticated route planner, high-quality cartography, and safety features like BuddyBeacon to ensure every trip is well-planned and safe.

How To Use

Explore the World in 6 Steps

1

Search Destinations

Browse officially recommended tours across 30+ outdoor activities.

2

Plan Your Route

Set your start and end points; the app calculates the best path automatically.

3

Check "Skyline"

Use Augmented Reality to identify peaks and landmarks around you.

4

Sync Devices

Connect with Garmin, Suunto, or Apple Watch for seamless tracking.

5

Enable BuddyBeacon

Share your real-time location with friends and family for added safety.

6

Rate & Review

Contribute to the community by sharing your own official-style tour reports.
