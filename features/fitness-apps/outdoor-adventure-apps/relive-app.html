What is Relive?

Bring Your Adventures to Life

Relive is a unique fitness app that creates personalized 3D videos of your outdoor activities. By combining GPS data with your photos, it generates a cinematic recap of your route, highlighting your pace, elevation, and the moments that mattered most along the way.

How To Use

Create Your Video in 6 Steps

1

Go Explore

Track your activity using the Relive app or connect your favorite tracker (like Garmin or Strava).

2

Finish & Save

Stop your recording once you've finished your hike, run, or cycle.

3

Add Your Photos

Select the best photos and videos from your trip to include in your 3D story.

4

Personalize

Add a title, notes, and choose the music that fits the vibe of your adventure.

5

Generate Video

Relive's engine will render your 3D route map and integrate your media.

6

Share with Friends

Download your video or share it directly to social media to inspire others.
