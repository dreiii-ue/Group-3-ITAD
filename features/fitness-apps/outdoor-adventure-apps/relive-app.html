Turn Every Ride.

Relive

What is Relive?

Visual Ride Tracking App

Relive helps cyclists record rides and transform them into animated visual stories using maps, stats, elevation, and ride photos. It combines activity tracking with storytelling, making it easier to relive, review, and share memorable outdoor experiences.

Editor's Rating: 4.4 / 5

How To Use

Start Using Relive in 6 Steps

1

Create an Account

Sign up and connect your preferred activity tracking app or device.

2

Record Your Ride

Track your cycling activity using Relive or a connected fitness app.

3

Add Photos

Upload ride photos to make your story more personal and engaging.

4

Generate Your Story

5

Evaluation

Pros & Cons

Pros

  • Creates engaging and easy-to-share ride stories.
  • Combines route data, stats, and media in one experience.
  • Great for cyclists who enjoy documenting their rides visually.
  • Simple concept that makes ride playback more interesting.

Cons

  • Less focused on advanced navigation than some cycling apps.
  • More useful for storytelling than route planning.
  • Users wanting deeper training metrics may need another app alongside it.
  • Some features may depend on connected services or premium access.

Verdict

Final Review

Relive is a great app for cyclists who want to make every ride feel more memorable. It is not mainly about deep route planning or advanced performance analysis. Instead, its strength is turning normal ride data into attractive visual stories that are fun to review and easy to share.
