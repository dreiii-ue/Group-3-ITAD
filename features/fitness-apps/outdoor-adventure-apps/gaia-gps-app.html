What is Gaia GPS?

Professional-Grade Backcountry Tools

Gaia GPS is a powerful navigation app designed for professionals and serious outdoor enthusiasts. It offers industry-leading map layers, from National Geographic trails to public/private land data, ensuring you have the tools to plan, track, and navigate any expedition.

How To Use

Master the Backcountry in 6 Steps

1

Select Map Layers

Choose between Topo, Satellite, or specialized layers like wildfire smoke.

2

Plan Your Route

Use the "Snap-to-Trail" feature on the web or mobile to create custom paths.

3

Download for Offline

Save high-resolution maps to your device to use when cell service is gone.

4

Track Your Location

View your precise GPS coordinates and altitude even in remote areas.

5

Mark Waypoints

Drop pins for campsites, water sources, or important landmarks.

6

Analyze Your Trip

Review your track logs, speed, and elevation profile after you return.
