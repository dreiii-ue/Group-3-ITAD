What is AllTrails?

Your Guide to the Great Outdoors

AllTrails is the world’s largest and most trusted outdoor platform. It provides detailed trail information for hiking, mountain biking, and running, empowering people to explore nature safely and sustainably with GPS navigation and community-sourced data.
