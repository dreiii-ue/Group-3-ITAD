What is AllTrails?

Your Guide to the Great Outdoors

AllTrails is the world’s largest and most trusted outdoor platform. It provides detailed trail information for hiking, mountain biking, and running, helping users explore nature safely with GPS navigation, offline trail access, and community-based updates.

Evaluation

Pros & Cons

Pros

  • Very easy to use for beginners and casual explorers.
  • Strong community-based reviews and trail condition updates.
  • Offline maps are useful for remote outdoor trips.
  • Supports multiple outdoor activities in one platform.

Cons

  • Some premium features are locked behind a subscription.
  • Trail quality may vary depending on local user updates.
  • Advanced users may want more detailed mapping layers.
  • Some areas have fewer route options than others.

Verdict

Final Review

AllTrails is one of the best apps for outdoor beginners and regular explorers. Its clean interface, strong trail discovery tools, and dependable community feedback make it a practical choice for planning and navigating outdoor adventures. It is especially useful for users who want simplicity, safety, and convenience in one place.
