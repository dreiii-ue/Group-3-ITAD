Hevy

What is Hevy?

A Smart Workout Tracker for Strength Training

Hevy is a workout tracking app used by lifters and gym enthusiasts to log exercises, monitor sets, reps, and weight, follow custom routines, and track strength progress over time. It combines detailed workout logging with community features to make resistance training more structured and motivating.

Editor's Rating: 4.6 / 5

Evaluation

Pros & Cons

Pros

  • Excellent for gym users focused on structured strength training.
  • Simple logging system makes it easy to record real-time workouts.
  • Progress tracking supports progressive overload and consistency.
  • Community aspect adds motivation without overwhelming the core workout tools.

Cons

  • Less useful for users who want guided workout videos or general wellness content.
  • Mainly focused on gym and strength training rather than broad fitness categories.
  • Some users may want even deeper analytics or coaching features.
  • Not the best fit for people who prefer fully guided beginner exercise programs.

Verdict

Final Review

Hevy is one of the best options for users who want a clean, focused workout tracker for strength training. It is especially useful for gym-goers who care about logging sessions properly, tracking overload, and maintaining routine consistency. It works best for lifters who want structure and measurable progress rather than general guided workouts.
