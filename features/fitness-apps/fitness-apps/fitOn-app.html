What is FitOn?

A Free Fitness App with Unlimited Guided Workouts

FitOn is a popular fitness application that provides free access to thousands of guided workout videos led by celebrity trainers and fitness experts. It covers a wide range of workout styles including HIIT, strength, yoga, Pilates, cardio, and meditation, making it one of the most versatile and accessible fitness platforms available for users of all fitness levels.

How To Use

Start Training with FitOn in 6 Simple Steps

1

Create an Account

Sign up for free and set up your fitness profile to begin accessing thousands of guided workout classes instantly.

2

Set Your Fitness Goals

Choose your goals such as losing weight, building strength, or reducing stress so FitOn can tailor recommendations for you.

3

Browse Workout Classes

Explore thousands of free classes across HIIT, yoga, strength, cardio, Pilates, dance, and mindfulness categories.

Train with Celebrity Trainers

Track Your Progress

Log completed workouts and monitor your weekly activity streaks to stay consistent and build lasting fitness habits.

Work Out with Friends

Invite friends to join live group workouts, share achievements, and keep each other accountable on your fitness journey.
