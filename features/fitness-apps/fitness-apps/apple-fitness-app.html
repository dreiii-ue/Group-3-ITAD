Apple Fitness+

What is Apple Fitness+?

A Premium Fitness Service Built Into the Apple Ecosystem

Apple Fitness+ is a subscription-based fitness service by Apple that delivers studio-quality workout videos, guided meditations, and wellness content directly to iPhone, iPad, and Apple TV. It integrates seamlessly with Apple Watch to display real-time metrics during workouts, making every session more personalized, motivating, and effective.

How To Use

Start Training with Apple Fitness+ in 6 Simple Steps

1

Subscribe to Fitness+

Sign up for Apple Fitness+ through your Apple ID and activate your subscription to unlock all workouts and content.

2

Pair Your Apple Watch

Connect your Apple Watch to display real-time heart rate, calories burned, and activity rings during every workout.

3

Choose a Workout

Browse a wide library of workout types including HIIT, yoga, strength, cycling, dance, and mindful cooldowns.

4

Train with Expert Instructors

Follow along with world-class trainers through high-production video sessions designed for all fitness levels.

5

Track Your Rings

Monitor your Move, Exercise, and Stand rings to stay consistent and hit your daily activity goals every day.

6

Build a Routine

Use custom plans and recommendations to establish a sustainable weekly workout schedule that fits your lifestyle.
