What is TrainerRoad?

A Science-Based Training Platform for Cyclists

TrainerRoad is a cycling training app built for riders who want to improve performance through structured workouts and personalized training plans. It supports indoor and outdoor training while helping cyclists analyze progress and prepare more effectively for events and goals.

How To Use

Start Training with TrainerRoad in 6 Simple Steps

1

Create an Account

Sign up and enter your cycling goals, experience, and preferred training schedule.

2

Choose a Training Plan

Select a plan that fits your fitness level and target event or cycling objective.

3

Connect Your Devices

Pair your smart trainer, heart rate monitor, or cycling computer for better data tracking.

4

Complete Your Workouts

Follow the scheduled sessions and stay consistent with your training routine.

5

Review Your Performance

Analyze workout data and track your improvement over time.

6

Adjust and Progress

Update your plan as your fitness develops and continue building cycling strength.
