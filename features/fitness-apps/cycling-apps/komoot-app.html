Komoot

What is Komoot?

A Smart Route Planner for Cyclists

Komoot is a cycling and outdoor navigation app that helps users find routes that fit their riding style. It allows cyclists to filter routes by distance and difficulty, explore new paths, and use navigation features for more confident rides.

How To Use

Start Riding with Komoot in 6 Simple Steps

1

Create an Account

Sign up and set your preferred cycling activity to personalize your route suggestions.

2

Search a Destination

Enter a place, trail, or region to start exploring cycling routes near you.

3

Choose Your Route

Filter routes by distance, surface, and difficulty to match your fitness level.

4

Save Offline Maps

Download your selected route so you can navigate even without mobile signal.

5

Follow Navigation

Use turn-by-turn guidance to stay on track during your ride.

6

Record and Share

Save your completed ride and share your route experience with others.
