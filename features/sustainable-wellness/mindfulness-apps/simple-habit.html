Simple Habit specializes in bite-sized, 5-minute meditation sessions designed to fit into the tightest schedules, perfect for professionals, students, and parents on the go.

Simple Habit

What is Simple Habit?

Quick, Effective Relief

Simple Habit provides high-impact mindfulness exercises tailored to specific moments in your day. By focusing on 5-minute sessions, it provides immediate stress reduction and mental clarity without requiring a major time commitment.

Editor's Rating: 4.6 / 5

How To Use

Build the Habit in 6 Steps

1

Find 5 Minutes

Carve out just a small window during a commute, break, or before bed.

2

Select Your Situation

Choose themes like "Tough Day at Work" or "Before a Meeting."

3

Follow the Guide

Listen to concise instructions from world-renowned mindfulness experts.

4

Take Series Courses

Commit to 7-day or 30-day series to build a lasting routine.

5

Use 'On the Go'

Quickly access offline meditations when you lack internet access.

6

Maintain the Streak

Use the tracker to ensure you hit your 5 minutes every single day.

Evaluation

Pros & Cons

Pros

  • Perfect for people who feel "too busy" to meditate.
  • The situational search makes finding relevant content easy.
  • Professional, calming voice talent and expert instruction.

Cons

  • Free version is quite limited compared to the premium plan.
  • 5-minute sessions might feel too short for advanced practitioners.
  • Subscription cost is higher than some competitors.

Verdict

Final Review

Simple Habit is the "coffee break" of meditation apps. It is designed for those who live in the fast lane and need quick mental resets. While it may lack the depth of longer unguided sessions, its situational library and expert-led 5-minute guides make it the best choice for professionals looking to maintain sanity in a high-stress environment.
