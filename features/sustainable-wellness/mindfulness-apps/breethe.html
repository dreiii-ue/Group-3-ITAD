What is Breethe?

Evaluation

Pros & Cons

Pros

  • Unique focus on hypnotherapy for tangible habit changes.
  • The "Inner Coach" helps users who don't know where to start.
  • High-quality production of soundscapes and sleep tales.
  • Content is organized by specific life situations (e.g., Flying).

Cons

  • The interface can feel slightly cluttered with many options.
  • Significant amount of content is locked behind the premium paywall.
  • Subscription cost is higher than some competitors.
  • Offline mode occasionally has loading issues.

Verdict

Final Review

Breethe is an excellent choice for those who find traditional meditation too "vague" and want actionable mental health tools. While it offers standard mindfulness, its real strength lies in the hypnotherapy sessions and expert masterclasses. If you are a busy professional or student looking for a "coach" in your pocket to help with specific anxieties or sleep issues, Breethe is worth the investment.
