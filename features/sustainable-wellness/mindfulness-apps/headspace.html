What is Headspace?

Guided Mindfulness for Everyone

Headspace is your comprehensive guide to mindfulness. Learn proven meditation techniques from world-class experts to transform your mental health, reduce stress, and find clarity.

How To Use Headspace

Get Started in 6 Simple Steps

1

Download the App

Get Headspace from the App Store or Google Play Store.

2

Create an Account

Sign up using your email and set up your basic wellness profile.

3

Choose Your Goal

Select areas to improve like sleep, stress, or focus during studies.

4

Start a Session

Try the 'Basics' course to learn the fundamentals of meditation.

5

Track Your Progress

Monitor your mindful minutes and see your journey build over time.

6

Build a Routine

Use daily reminders to make mental wellness a consistent habit.
