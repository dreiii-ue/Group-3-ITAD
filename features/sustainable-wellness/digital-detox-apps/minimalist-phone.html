Minimalist Phone

What is Minimalist Phone?

A Launcher That Fights Back

Minimalist Phone is a specialized launcher for Android that strips away colorful, attention-grabbing icons and replaces them with a clean, monochrome, list-based UI. By removing visual triggers, it significantly reduces the dopamine hits that keep you glued to your screen.


How To Use

Transform Your Device in 6 Steps

1

Set as Default

Install and set Minimalist Phone as your default home screen launcher.

2

Hide Distractions

Hide apps you don't need daily to keep your interface clean.

3

Enable In-App Timers

Set time limits for specific apps that trigger a block once reached.

4

Monochrome Mode

Apply a grayscale filter to make the entire phone less stimulating.

5

Filter Notifications

Use the Notification Filter to batch non-urgent pings for later.

6

Enjoy Clarity

Focus on your tasks without the constant pull of colorful app icons.

Why Users Switch to Minimalism

Zero Icon Friction

Eliminating colorful logos removes the subconscious urge to tap on social media apps.

Mindful Delay

Optional "open delays" force you to wait a few seconds before an app opens, killing impulsive usage.

Mental Clarity

A text-based layout helps your brain stay calm and focused rather than constantly overstimulated.
