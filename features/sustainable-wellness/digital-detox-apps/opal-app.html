What is Opal?

Your Phone's Built-In Willpower

Opal is a powerful app-blocking tool that shields your focus by locking distracting apps during deep work sessions. It uses behavioral science to break scroll addiction and help you build healthier digital habits. Whether you're studying for exams, finishing a project, or just trying to be more present, Opal creates an unbreakable barrier between you and distraction — so you can actually finish what you start.
