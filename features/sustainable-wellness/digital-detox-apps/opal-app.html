Evaluation

Pros & Cons

Pros

  • Extremely difficult to "cheat" compared to basic Screen Time settings.
  • Beautiful, modern UI that makes tracking focus enjoyable.
  • Excellent whitelist system allows essential apps (like Maps or Music).
  • Weekly reports provide clear proof of productivity gains.

Cons

  • Premium subscription is relatively expensive for students.
  • Deep Focus can be scary—if you lock yourself out, you stay out.
  • Android version is still catching up to the features of the iOS app.
  • Initial setup requires granting several system permissions.

Verdict

Final Review

Opal is the definitive tool for anyone who feels their phone is winning the war for their attention. While basic app timers are easy to ignore, Opal's system-level blocking provides the firm boundary many of us need to actually get work done. It is particularly effective for students and remote workers who struggle with "quick checks" that turn into hour-long scrolls.
