What is Freedom?

The Cross-Platform Focus App Built for Serious Students

Freedom is a productivity app that blocks distracting websites and apps simultaneously across all your devices — in one click. Whether you're on your phone, tablet, laptop, or desktop, Freedom synchronizes your blocking sessions so there's no easy way out. With over 2.5 million members and more than 10 years in the market, Freedom is trusted by students, writers, developers, and creators who need real, no-compromise focus time.
