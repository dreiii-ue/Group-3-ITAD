What is Flipd?

Stay Off Your Phone. Together.

Flipd is a focus and accountability app designed for students and professionals. It lets you lock out distracting apps, join group study rooms, and track your screen-free time — all while connecting with a community of focused peers working toward the same goals.

How To Use

Focus Smarter in 6 Steps

1

Download Flipd

Install Flipd for free on iOS or Android and create your account.

2

Set a Focus Timer

Choose how long you want to stay phone-free — from 5 minutes to hours.

3

Lock Your Screen

Activate Full Lock mode to block all apps and keep distractions away.

4

Join a Study Room

Hop into a live virtual study session and focus alongside real people.

5

Track Your Streaks

Watch your phone-free minutes accumulate and build daily streaks.

6

Review Progress

Check weekly reports to see how much screen-free time you've reclaimed.
