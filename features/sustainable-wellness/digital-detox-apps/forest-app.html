What is Forest?

A Lush Solution to Phone Addiction

Forest is a gamified timer that encourages users to stay off their phones. When you want to focus, you plant a seed. While you work, the seed grows into a tree. If you leave the app to check social media, your tree withers. Successful sessions earn you coins to plant real trees through our environmental partners.

How To Use

Cultivate Concentration in 6 Steps

1

Set Your Time

Choose a focus duration from 10 to 120 minutes.

2

Plant a Seed

Choose your favorite tree species and start the timer.

3

Stay Focused

Keep the app open. Your tree grows as long as you concentrate.

4

Avoid Distractions

Leaving the app before time is up will cause your tree to wither.

5

Build Your Forest

Successful sessions add unique trees to your daily overview.

6

Plant Real Trees

Use your virtual coins to sponsor real tree planting globally.
