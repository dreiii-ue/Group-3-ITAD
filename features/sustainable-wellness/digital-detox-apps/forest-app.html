Forest

What is Forest?

A Lush Solution to Phone Addiction

Forest is a gamified timer that encourages users to stay productive. When you want to focus, you plant a seed that grows into a tree while you work.

Editor's Rating: 4.8 / 5

How To Use

Cultivate Concentration in 6 Steps

1

Set Your Time

Choose a focus duration from 10 to 120 minutes.

2

Plant a Seed

Choose your favorite tree species and start the timer.

3

Stay Focused

Keep the app open. Your tree grows as long as you concentrate.

4

Avoid Distractions

Leaving the app will cause your tree to wither and die.

5

Build Your Forest

Successful sessions add unique trees to your daily overview.

6

Plant Real Trees

Use virtual coins to sponsor real reforestation globally.

Evaluation

Pros & Cons

Pros

  • Highly motivating visual rewards for staying off your phone.
  • Positive real-world environmental impact through "Trees for the Future."
  • Beautifully designed user interface that is easy to navigate.
  • Cross-platform sync between mobile and browser extensions.

Cons

  • The mobile app requires a small one-time purchase on some platforms.
  • Real tree planting is limited by the amount of coins you can earn.
  • Whitelist settings can be tricky to set up for necessary apps.
  • No built-in task manager or to-do list.

Verdict

Final Review

Forest is the gold standard for digital detox and focus apps. Its clever use of "loss aversion"—the idea that you don't want to kill a virtual tree—is surprisingly effective at keeping users away from distracting social media. While it lacks some advanced project management tools, its simplicity and eco-conscious mission make it an essential tool for students and professionals alike.
