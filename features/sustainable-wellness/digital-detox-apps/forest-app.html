What is Forest?

The World's Most Eco-Friendly Focus App

Forest is a gamified focus app where your digital habits have real-world environmental impact. Plant a virtual tree when you start a session — it grows while you stay focused, and dies if you leave the app. Earn virtual coins and redeem them to sponsor actual tree-planting projects through their non-profit partner Trees for the Future. With over 10 million downloads and more than 1 million real trees planted, Forest proves that small daily habits create big change.

How To Use

Get Started in 6 Simple Steps

1

Download Forest

Free on Android, available on iOS and as a Chrome extension.

2

Pick a Tree Species

Choose from dozens of tree types, or unlock more as your forest grows.

3

Set a Timer

Choose your focus length — from 10 minutes up to 2 hours.

4

Start Planting

Hit plant and keep Forest open. Your tree grows in real time as you focus.

5

Don't Leave the App!

Opening another app kills your tree. The guilt is a surprisingly effective deterrent.

6

Plant Real Trees

Spend your earned coins to sponsor actual tree-planting in Africa and beyond.
