Tellmi

What is Tellmi?

A Safe Space for Real Conversations

Tellmi (formerly MeeToo) is an award-winning anonymous app that provides a safe space for people to share their problems. Every post and reply is moderated by humans to ensure a positive environment, making peer support safe and reliable.

Editor's Rating: 4.9 / 5

How To Use

Connect with Your Peers in 6 Simple Steps

1

Sign Up Anonymously

Create an account with just your age and a username; your identity stays private.

2

Tell Your Story

Write a post about what’s on your mind—stress, anxiety, or life challenges.

3

Human Moderation

Wait for moderators to check your post for safety before it goes live to the community.

4

Receive Support

Get empathetic advice and encouragement from others who have been through similar situations.

5

Give Support

Browse through posts and offer kind words or helpful tips to others in need.

6

Access Resources

Check the "Directory" for expert advice and direct links to professional help.

Evaluation

Pros & Cons

Pros

  • Incredible safety standards due to 100% human moderation.
  • Creates a genuine sense of belonging and community.
  • Free to use with no hidden costs or intrusive advertisements.
  • Validated by clinical research for its positive impact.

Cons

  • Moderation can lead to slight delays in posts appearing.
  • Not a replacement for professional therapy or crisis intervention.
  • Age-gated community means you only see relevant age groups.
  • Text-based only; no voice or video support features.

Verdict

Final Review

Tellmi is quite possibly the safest social network on the planet. By prioritizing human moderation over algorithms, it creates a sanctuary for those struggling with mental health. It successfully bridges the gap between isolation and therapy by providing a community that truly listens. For anyone feeling alone in their struggles, Tellmi is an essential download.
