Tellmi is a safe, anonymous peer-support community where you can talk about anything from mental health to daily struggles without fear of judgment.

Tellmi

What is Tellmi?

A Safe Space for Real Conversations

Tellmi (formerly MeeToo) is an award-winning anonymous app that provides a safe space for people to share their problems and support others. Every post and reply is moderated by humans to ensure a positive environment, making peer support safe, reliable, and accessible.

How To Use

Connect with Your Peers in 6 Simple Steps

1

Sign Up Anonymously

Create an account with just your age and a username; your real identity stays private.

2

Tell Your Story

Write a post about what’s on your mind—whether it's stress, anxiety, or life challenges.

3

Human Moderation

Wait a few moments for the moderators to check your post for safety before it goes live.

4

Receive Support

Get empathetic advice and encouragement from others who have been through similar situations.

5

Give Support

Browse through posts and offer kind words or helpful tips to others in the community.

6

Access Resources

Check the "Directory" for expert advice and direct links to professional help if needed.
