What is Pacer?

More Than Just a Pedometer

Pacer is a comprehensive health and fitness app that automatically records your steps 24/7. Beyond step counting, it offers GPS-tracked workouts, personalized fitness plans, and social challenges to keep you moving.

Editor's Rating: 4.7 / 5

How To Use

Step Into Fitness in 6 Simple Steps

1

Download & Sync

Install the app and allow access to motion sensors to track steps automatically.

2

Set Your Daily Goal

Define your target step count based on your current fitness level and goals.

3

Keep Your Phone Close

Pacer works in the background even when the screen is off while in your pocket.

4

Log Outdoor Workouts

Use "GPS" mode to track specific walks, runs, or bike rides with detailed maps.

5

Join a Challenge

Participate in virtual challenges with the community to stay motivated and earn badges.

6

Monitor Health Trends

Review data patterns in your activity, weight, and BMI over weeks and months.

Evaluation

Pros & Cons

Pros

  • No extra hardware required; works purely with your smartphone.
  • Very accurate step counting compared to other free apps.
  • Vibrant social community provides excellent accountability.
  • Simple, intuitive interface that is easy for all ages to navigate.

Cons

  • The free version includes ads which can be distracting.
  • Advanced training plans and deep analytics require Pacer Premium.
  • GPS accuracy can vary depending on your phone's hardware.
  • Requires you to carry your phone at all times for step credit.

Verdict

Final Review

Pacer is an exceptional entry-point for anyone wanting to track their fitness without buying a wearable. It excels at providing a "pedometer in your pocket" experience while offering enough social and GPS features to grow with you. While the ads in the free version are a minor hurdle, the community challenges and holistic health tracking make it a top-tier choice for weight loss and activity monitoring.
