Pacer

What is Pacer?

More Than Just a Pedometer

Pacer is a comprehensive health and fitness app that automatically records your steps 24/7. Beyond step counting, it offers GPS-tracked workouts, personalized fitness plans, and social challenges to keep you moving and motivated throughout the day.

How To Use

Step Into Fitness in 6 Simple Steps

1

Download & Sync

Install the app and allow it to access your phone's motion sensors to track steps automatically.

2

Set Your Daily Goal

Define your target step count based on your current fitness level and long-term objectives.

3

Keep Your Phone Close

Keep your phone in your pocket or hand; Pacer works in the background even when the screen is off.

4

Log Outdoor Workouts

Use the "GPS" mode to track your specific walks, runs, or bike rides with detailed maps.

5

Join a Challenge

Participate in virtual walking challenges with friends or the global community to earn badges.

6

Monitor Health Trends

Review your weekly and monthly data to see patterns in your activity, weight, and BMI.
