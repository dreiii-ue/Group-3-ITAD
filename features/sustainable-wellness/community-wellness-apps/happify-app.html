What is Happify?

Science-Based Games for Happiness

Happify is a wellness platform that uses "happiness tracks"—sets of games and activities based on Positive Psychology, CBT, and Mindfulness. It is designed to rewire your brain to focus on the positive, helping you manage stress and build emotional strength through play.

How To Use

Boost Your Mood in 6 Simple Steps

1

Complete Your Assessment

Answer a few simple questions to determine your current "Happiness Score" and goals.

2

Choose a Track

Select a personalized track, such as "Coping with Stress" or "Building Self-Confidence."

3

Play Daily Games

Engage in short, 5-minute games designed to train your brain toward positive thinking.

4

Complete Activities

Follow prompts for real-world actions, like practicing gratitude or acts of kindness.

5

Earn Rewards

Collect points and unlock new levels as you consistently practice your emotional skills.

6

Check Your Progress

Retake the assessment every two weeks to see how your happiness skills have improved.
