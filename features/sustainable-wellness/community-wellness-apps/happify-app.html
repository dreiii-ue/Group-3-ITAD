What is Happify?

Science-Based Games for Happiness

Happify is a wellness platform that uses "happiness tracks"—sets of games designed to rewire your brain to focus on the positive and manage stress.

Evaluation

Pros & Cons

Pros

  • Highly engaging gamified interface makes daily use enjoyable.
  • Personalized tracks cater to specific mental health goals.
  • Based on credible research in CBT and Mindfulness.
  • Excellent for building long-term positive habits.

Cons

  • Free version offers limited access to the full library of tracks.
  • The gamified approach might feel too "casual" for some users.
  • Requires consistent daily commitment to see measurable results.
  • Subscription cost for Happify Plus is required for full features.

Verdict

Final Review

Happify is a standout wellness tool for anyone looking to improve their mental outlook. Its detailed "happiness tracks"—which intelligently combine CBT, mindfulness, and play—make it superior to standard meditation apps. While the subscription is needed for the full experience, the investment is worthwhile for anyone looking to build emotional resilience through an interactive and supportive platform.
