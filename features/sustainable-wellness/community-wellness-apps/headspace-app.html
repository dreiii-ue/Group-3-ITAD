What is Headspace?

A Digital Guide to Mental Wellness

Headspace is a world-leading meditation and mindfulness app designed to make mental health accessible to everyone. Through science-backed tools and guided sessions, it helps users manage anxiety, build resilience, and maintain a healthier, happier state of mind.

How To Use

Master Mindfulness in 6 Simple Steps

1

Pick Your Goal

Choose what you want to focus on: sleeping better, reducing stress, or improving focus.

2

Start with Basics

If you're new, begin with the "Basics" course to learn the fundamentals of meditation.

3

Find a Quiet Space

Set aside just 5-10 minutes in a comfortable spot where you won't be interrupted.

4

Listen & Follow

Play a guided session and follow the narrator's instructions on breathing and awareness.

5

Explore Sleepcasts

Use "Sleepcasts" or calming audio landscapes to help you drift off into a restful slumber.

6

Track Your Journey

Check your "Mindful Minutes" and streaks to see how your practice grows over time.
