WellTrack provides a suite of online tools based on Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) to help you manage stress, anxiety, and depression through data-driven insights.

WellTrack

What is WellTrack?

Personalized Mental Wellness Tools

WellTrack is an interactive self-help platform that helps users monitor their mental health. It uses clinical assessments and mood tracking to identify patterns in your behavior, providing customized tools like the Zen Room, MoodCheck, and Thought Diary to improve your well-being.

How To Use

Manage Your Wellness in 6 Simple Steps

1

Take the Assessment

Start with a "Wellness Assessment" to gauge your current levels of stress, anxiety, and depression.

2

Daily MoodCheck

Quickly log your mood throughout the day to track how your feelings change over time.

3

Identify Triggers

Use the "Activity Scheduler" to see which activities or locations correlate with your best and worst moods.

4

Challenge Your Thoughts

Utilize the "Thought Diary" to identify and reframe negative thought patterns into positive ones.

5

Visit the Zen Room

Access relaxation tools and guided muscle relaxation sessions to decompress during stressful moments.

6

Track Your Growth

Review your progress reports regularly to see how your mental health scores improve over weeks of use.
