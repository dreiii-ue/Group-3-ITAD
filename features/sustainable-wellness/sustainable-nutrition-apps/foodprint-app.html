What is FoodPrint™?

A Smart Nutrition Tracker Built Around Your Lifestyle

FoodPrint™ is an intelligent nutrition tracking application that lets you log meals, monitor calories, and track both macronutrients and micronutrients with ease. Using a photo-first logging experience, a barcode scanner, and a vast food database, FoodPrint gives you a complete picture of your daily nutrition while also tracking workouts, sleep, and lifestyle habits — all in one place.

Editor's Rating: 4.2 / 5

How To Use

Start Tracking Your Nutrition with FoodPrint™ in 6 Simple Steps

1

Create an Account

Download the app and set up your profile by entering your age, weight, height, and health goals to personalize your nutrition targets.

2

Set Your Nutrition Goals

Define your calorie budget and choose whether to focus on weight loss, muscle gain, or general wellness so the app can tailor your daily targets.

3

Log Your Meals

Snap a photo of your food and it syncs directly into your diary. Search the extensive database or use the barcode scanner for packaged items.

4

Track Macros & Micros

Monitor your daily intake of calories, carbs, protein, and fat — plus micronutrients like iron, fiber, B12, and Vitamin D for a full nutritional breakdown.

5

Log Your Lifestyle

Record workouts, sleep, and medications all within the same platform. FoodPrint even supports blood glucose and insulin logging for those managing diabetes.

6

Connect Your Devices

Sync with Fitbit, Oura, Garmin, Withings, or Apple Health to automatically update your activity and sleep data inside the FoodPrint app.

Evaluation

Pros & Cons

Pros

  • Photo-first meal logging makes tracking fast, frictionless, and visually intuitive.
  • Comprehensive lifestyle tracking — workouts, sleep, and medications all in one app.
  • Supports blood glucose and insulin logging, making it accessible for diabetic users.
  • Strong wearable device integration with Fitbit, Oura, Garmin, and Apple Health.

Cons

  • Photo recognition accuracy can vary — manual corrections are sometimes needed.
  • Smaller overall user base compared to more established nutrition tracking apps.
  • Premium features are locked behind a subscription paywall.
  • Food database, while extensive, may lack some regional or local food items.

Verdict

Final Review

FoodPrint™ is a smart and accessible nutrition tracker that excels with its photo-first logging experience and broad lifestyle tracking capabilities. It's particularly well-suited for users who want a visual, low-friction way to monitor their diet alongside workouts, sleep, and health metrics. While photo recognition isn't perfect and a subscription is needed for the full experience, FoodPrint offers a genuinely modern approach to nutrition tracking. Recommended for health-conscious individuals who want a holistic, all-in-one daily wellness companion.
