Eat This Much

What is Eat This Much?

An Automatic Meal Planner Built Around Your Goals

Eat This Much is an intelligent meal planning application that automatically generates full daily and weekly meal plans based on your calorie targets, dietary preferences, food restrictions, and nutritional goals. It takes the guesswork out of eating well by creating personalized menus, generating grocery lists, and tracking your macros and nutrients all in one place.

How To Use

Start Meal Planning with Eat This Much in 6 Simple Steps

1

Create an Account

Sign up and set up your nutrition profile by entering your age, weight, height, and health goals to get started.

2

Set Your Dietary Preferences

Choose your diet type, food restrictions, allergies, and cuisine preferences so the app can tailor meal plans just for you.

3

Generate Your Meal Plan

Let the app automatically create a full day or week of balanced meals that match your calorie and macro targets.

4

Customize Your Meals

Swap out meals or individual ingredients you dislike and regenerate alternatives that still meet your nutritional goals.

5

Get Your Grocery List

Export an auto-generated shopping list based on your meal plan so you always have exactly what you need on hand.

6

Track Your Nutrition

Log your meals and monitor your daily calories, macros, and micronutrients to stay on track with your health goals.
