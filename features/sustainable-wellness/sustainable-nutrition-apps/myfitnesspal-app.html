What is MyFitnessPal?

A Smarter Way to Manage Your Diet & Fitness

MyFitnessPal is an all-in-one calorie counter and fitness tracker that helps users reach their health goals. Log your meals using the world's largest food database with over 14 million items, track your exercise, and monitor your macros—all in one place. Whether your goal is weight loss, muscle gain, or simply eating healthier, MyFitnessPal gives you the data to make it happen.

How To Use

Hit Your Goals with MyFitnessPal in 6 Simple Steps

1

Create an Account

Sign up and build your profile by entering your age, weight, height, and health goal to get a personalized calorie target.

2

Set Your Goals

Choose your objective—lose weight, maintain, or build muscle—and let MyFitnessPal calculate your daily calorie and macro targets.

3

Log Your Meals

Search or scan barcodes to instantly add foods from a database of over 14 million items, including restaurant meals and branded products.

4

Track Your Macros

Monitor calories, protein, carbs, fats, and micronutrients throughout the day with easy-to-read charts and nutrient breakdowns.

5

Log Your Workouts

Add cardio or strength training sessions to automatically adjust your calorie budget and keep your net intake on target.

6

Review Your Progress

Use trend reports and progress charts to see how your nutrition and habits evolve over time and stay motivated.
