What is Yuka?

An Independent Product Scanner Built Around Your Health

Yuka is a free, independent mobile application that lets you scan the barcodes of food and personal care products to instantly see their health impact. Each product is evaluated based on nutritional quality, the presence of additives, and its organic status — and scored on a clear 0–100 scale with a color-coded rating from Excellent to Bad. When a product scores poorly, Yuka recommends a healthier alternative, all with zero influence from brands or advertisers.

How To Use

Start Scanning Smarter with Yuka in 6 Simple Steps

1

Download the App

Install Yuka for free on iOS or Android — no account required to start scanning food and cosmetic products right away.

2

Scan a Product Barcode

Open the app and tap the scanner icon, then point your camera at any product's barcode to get an instant health analysis.

3

Read the Health Score

View the product's score from 0 to 100 along with a color-coded rating — Excellent, Good, Poor, or Bad — for an immediate health verdict.

4

Review the Detailed Breakdown

Dive into the full data sheet to see a product's nutritional quality, additive risks, and organic status — with clear explanations of each factor.

5

Discover Healthier Alternatives

If a product scores poorly, Yuka independently recommends a similar product with a better health score so you can make a smarter swap on the spot.

6

Build Your Favorites List

Save your trusted clean products to your favorites and revisit your scan history so healthy shopping becomes faster every time.
