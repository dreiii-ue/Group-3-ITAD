What is Cronometer?

A Precision Nutrition Tracker Built for Serious Health Goals

Cronometer is a highly accurate nutrition tracking application that logs calories, macronutrients, and over 80 micronutrients including vitamins, minerals, and amino acids. Trusted by dietitians, athletes, and health-conscious individuals, it uses verified food data from authoritative sources like the USDA and NCCDB. Whether you're managing a health condition, optimizing performance, or simply eating better, Cronometer gives you the precision to make truly informed decisions.

How To Use

Start Tracking Your Nutrition with Cronometer in 6 Simple Steps

1

Create an Account

Sign up on the app or website and set up your profile by entering your age, weight, height, and health goals to personalize your nutrition targets.

2

Set Your Nutrition Goals

Define your calorie budget and macro targets, or choose a preset diet like keto, vegan, or high-protein so Cronometer can tailor your daily nutrient goals.

3

Log Your Meals

Search the verified food database, scan barcodes, or add custom recipes to quickly log every meal, snack, and drink throughout your day.

4

Track Macros & Micros

Monitor calories, carbs, protein, and fat — plus over 80 micronutrients like calcium, magnesium, zinc, B12, and Vitamin K for a truly complete nutritional breakdown.

5

Log Biometrics & Exercise

Record your weight, blood pressure, blood glucose, and workouts to track how your diet and lifestyle habits affect your overall health over time.

6

Connect Your Devices

Sync with Apple Health, Fitbit, Garmin, Oura, and more to automatically import activity and biometric data directly into your Cronometer diary.
