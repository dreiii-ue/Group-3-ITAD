Evaluation

Pros & Cons

Pros

  • Scientifically structured programs rooted in progressive overload principles.
  • Unique Strength Score metric for objective progress measurement.
  • Ad-free experience with no interruptions during training.
  • Comprehensive exercise video library for proper form guidance.

Cons

  • Coaching tier requires a paid subscription for full access.
  • Limited cardio and non-strength activity tracking.
  • Smaller user community compared to mainstream fitness apps.
  • Interface may feel data-heavy for casual or beginner users.

Verdict

Final Review

Caliber is a standout choice for anyone serious about strength training. Its evidence-based programming, automatic progressive overload, and objective Strength Score system make it far more sophisticated than generic workout trackers. While the coaching tier requires a subscription, the free version alone delivers exceptional value. Highly recommended for intermediate to advanced lifters who want a data-driven approach to building real, lasting strength.
