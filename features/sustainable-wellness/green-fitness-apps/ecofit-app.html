What is Ecofit?

The Leader in Sustainable Wellness

Ecofit is a specialized green fitness app that rewards users for sustainable movement. By integrating with your daily activity, it provides carbon offset tracking, environmental challenges, and a marketplace for eco-friendly products.

How To Use

Get Started in 6 Simple Steps
