What is EcoFit?

The App That Rewards Green Exercise

EcoFit is a fitness tracker designed for the eco-conscious individual, helping you stay fit while actively reducing your carbon footprint.

How To Use

Start Your Eco-Journey in 6 Steps

1

Sign Up

Create your profile and select your preferred environmental causes.

2

Sync Wearables

Connect your smartwatch or phone sensors for automatic tracking.

3

Choose Green Travel

Walk or cycle to earn "Eco-Points" based on CO2 saved.

4

Complete Challenges

Join community events to reach collective sustainability goals.

5

Redeem Impact

Use points to plant trees or support renewable energy projects.

6

Analyze Statistics

Review your monthly impact report and fitness progress.
