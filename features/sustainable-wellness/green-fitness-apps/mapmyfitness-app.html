Map My Fitness

What is Map My Fitness?

Personalized GPS Activity Tracking

Map My Fitness by Under Armour is a comprehensive fitness ecosystem that tracks over 600 activities with precise GPS, providing detailed feedback on pace, distance, and elevation.

Editor's Rating: 4.3 / 5

How To Use

Start Mapping in 6 Simple Steps

1

Download & Register

Get the app on iOS or Android and create your Under Armour account.

2

Connect Your Gear

Sync your UA HOVR shoes or Apple Watch for better data accuracy.

3

Select Activity

Choose from over 600 sport types to ensure accurate tracking metrics.

4

Record Your Workout

Hit 'Start' and let GPS map your route while providing audio coaching.

5

Analyze Your Stats

Review your splits, elevation gain, and intensity levels after every session.

6

Join the Community

Share your routes and participate in global fitness challenges with others.

Evaluation

Pros & Cons

Pros

  • Tracks over 600 different activity types for exceptional versatility.
  • Deep integration with Under Armour gear and the wider UA ecosystem.
  • Unique shoe mileage tracker helps prevent injury from worn-out footwear.
  • Large community with millions of shared routes across the globe.

Cons

  • Most advanced training plans and analytics locked behind MVP subscription.
  • Battery drain can be significant during long GPS-tracked sessions.
  • Interface can feel cluttered with Under Armour product promotions.
  • Occasional GPS accuracy issues reported in urban environments.

Verdict

Final Review

Map My Fitness is a powerhouse GPS tracker for multi-sport athletes. Its unrivaled activity variety, real-time audio coaching, and smart gear-tracking features make it a compelling choice for runners, cyclists, and outdoor enthusiasts alike. While the premium features sit behind a paywall, the free tier still delivers tremendous value. A reliable, feature-rich choice for anyone serious about tracking their active lifestyle.
