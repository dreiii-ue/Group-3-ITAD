What is Map My Fitness?

Personalized GPS Tracking

Map My Fitness by Under Armour is a comprehensive fitness tracking ecosystem. It allows users to track over 600 different types of activities, from running and cycling to gym workouts and cross-training. By utilizing GPS technology, it provides detailed feedback on pace, distance, duration, and calorie burn, while connecting you to a global community of fitness enthusiasts.

How To Use

Start Mapping in 6 Simple Steps

1

Download & Register

Get the app on iOS or Android and create your Under Armour account.

2

Connect Gear

Sync your UA HOVR shoes, Apple Watch, or heart rate monitor for better data.

3

Select Activity

4

Hit 'Start' and let GPS map your route while providing real-time audio coaching.

5

Analyze Your Stats

Review your splits, elevation gain, and intensity levels after every session.

6

Join the Community

Share your routes, participate in challenges, and motivate friends.
