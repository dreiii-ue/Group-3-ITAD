Map My Fitness

What is Map My Fitness?

Personalized GPS Activity Tracking

Map My Fitness by Under Armour is a comprehensive fitness ecosystem. It allows users to track over 600 different types of activities, providing detailed feedback on pace, distance, and duration using precise GPS technology.

How To Use

Start Mapping in 6 Simple Steps

1

Download & Register

Get the app on iOS or Android and create your Under Armour account.

2

Connect Your Gear

Sync your UA HOVR shoes or Apple Watch for better data accuracy.

3

Select Activity

Choose from over 600 sport types to ensure accurate tracking metrics.

4

Record Your Workout

Hit 'Start' and let GPS map your route while providing audio coaching.

5

Analyze Your Stats

Review your splits, elevation gain, and intensity levels after every session.

6

Join the Community

Share your routes and participate in global fitness challenges with others.
