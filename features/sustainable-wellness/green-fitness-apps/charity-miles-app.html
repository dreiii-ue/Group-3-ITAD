Charity Miles is a socially-driven fitness app that turns your miles into money for top charities. Every step you take helps create a positive impact in the world.

Charity Miles

What is Charity Miles?

The App That Gives Back

Charity Miles is an award-winning app that enables you to earn money for charity while you walk, run, or bike. Sponsored by corporate partners, the app converts your distance into donations for over 40 world-class charities, making every workout a selfless act of kindness.

How To Use

Make Your Miles Matter in 6 Steps

1

Install the App

Download Charity Miles from the App Store or Google Play for free.

2

Choose Your Charity

Select from 40+ organizations like ALS Association, Stand Up To Cancer, or WWF.

3

Select Activity

Pick your movement: walking, running, or cycling (indoors or outdoors).

4

Start Your Session

Hit the start button and keep your phone with you as you move.

5

Track Your Impact

See exactly how many miles you've logged and the money raised for your cause.

6

Share Your Progress

Post your workout to social media to spread awareness and inspire others.
