Fitness for a Cause

Move for Charity.
Change the World.

Charity Miles is a free app that enables you to earn money for world-changing charities whenever you walk, run, or bike. By turning your everyday miles into corporate-sponsored donations, the app helps you stay active while making a tangible social impact with every step.

Charity Miles

What is Charity Miles?

Turning Fitness into Philanthropy

Charity Miles allows users to select a cause they care about and earn funds for it through physical activity, supported by corporate sponsors who donate for every mile logged.

How To Use

Make an Impact in 6 Steps

1

Download & Sign Up

Install the app and create your profile to start tracking.

2

Choose Your Charity

Select from over 40 world-changing organizations to support.

3

Select Activity

Choose whether you are walking, running, or cycling.

4

Sync Your Devices

Connect Strava or Health Kit to track all your daily miles.

5

Complete Your Move

Log your workout distance to trigger a sponsor donation.

6

Share & Inspire

Share your progress on social media to raise more awareness.
