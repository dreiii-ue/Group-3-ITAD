Good On You

What is Good On You?

The Ethical Shopping Assistant

Good On You uses expert analysis to give thousands of fashion brands an easy-to-understand score based on labor rights, environmental impact, and animal welfare.

Editor's Rating: 4.4 / 5

How To Use

Shop Sustainably in 6 Steps

1

Search a Brand

Type in your favorite clothing brand to see its current ethical rating from "Great" to "We Avoid."

2

Check the Breakdown

See specific scores for People (Labor), Planet (Environment), and Animals.

3

Find Alternatives

If a brand scores poorly, use the app to discover "Better Alternatives" that match your style.

4

Read Ethical Guides

Browse articles on sustainable materials, circular fashion, and how to spot greenwashing.

5

Filter by Values

Filter searches for brands that are Vegan, Fair Trade, or use Organic materials.

6

Shop with Confidence

Use the curated "Best Of" lists to find sustainable options for every occasion.

App Capabilities

Core Features

Brand Rating System

A five-tier rating scale from "Great" to "We Avoid" makes understanding a brand's ethics instant and clear.

Values-Based Filters

Filter thousands of brands by Vegan, Fair Trade, Organic, or B-Corp certified status to find your perfect match.

Better Alternatives

Instantly discover higher-rated brands in the same style category when a favourite scores poorly.

Editorial Content

Access in-depth guides on sustainable materials, greenwashing red flags, and ethical shopping tips.

Benefits

Why Ethical Shoppers Choose It

Transparency

Access clear data on how brands treat their workers and manage their supply chains.

Eco-Conscious

Easily identify brands using sustainable energy and reducing water waste in production.

Animal Welfare

Filter for cruelty-free and vegan brands that don't use fur, leather, or animal testing.

Evaluation

Pros & Cons

Pros

  • Covers thousands of brands with a clear, easy-to-read rating system.
  • Breaks down scores into People, Planet, and Animal categories.
  • Provides alternatives so you never feel stuck with a bad brand.
  • Rich editorial content helps educate users on sustainable fashion.

Cons

  • Many smaller or regional brands are not yet rated in the database.
  • Ratings are not always updated in real-time as brand practices change.
  • Premium features and full directory access require a paid subscription.
  • Methodology can be complex to understand for casual users.

Verdict

Final Review

Good On You is the gold standard for anyone wanting to align their wardrobe with their values. Its comprehensive, three-dimensional rating system cuts through greenwashing and delivers genuinely actionable information at a glance. While the database doesn't cover every brand and some ratings may lag behind real-world changes, it remains an indispensable tool for conscious consumers. For anyone trying to move away from fast fashion toward a more ethical wardrobe, this app is an essential starting point.
