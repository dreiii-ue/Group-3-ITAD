Sustainable Web Browsing

Search the Web.
Plant Trees.

Ecosia is a search engine that uses its ad revenue to plant trees where nature and people need them most, allowing you to fight climate change just by surfing the web.

Ecosia

What is Ecosia?

The Eco-Friendly Search Engine

Ecosia works just like Google, but with a major difference: 100% of its profits go towards climate action, with the majority dedicated to planting trees around the world.

Editor's Rating: 4.7 / 5

How To Use

Make an Impact in 6 Steps

1

Get the App or Extension

Download Ecosia on iOS/Android or add the free extension to Chrome, Safari, or Edge.

2

Set as Default

Make Ecosia your default search engine so every query automatically contributes to the planet.

3

Search Normally

Browse the web, find answers, look up images, and read the news just like you normally would.

4

Watch Your Counter

Keep an eye on the personal tree counter in the top right corner to see your direct impact.

5

Learn About Projects

Click the tree icon to explore global reforestation projects funded by your searches.

6

Browse Privately

Search with peace of mind knowing Ecosia protects your privacy and doesn't sell your data.

Evaluation

Pros & Cons

Pros

  • Free to use — no subscription or cost required.
  • Transparent financial reports show exactly where revenue goes.
  • Strong privacy stance with no data selling or user profiling.
  • Available as a browser extension and mobile app on all platforms.

Cons

  • Search results are not always as relevant as Google's.
  • Powered by Bing, which may limit result quality for niche queries.
  • Tree planting impact depends on ad revenue, which can fluctuate.
  • Limited advanced search filters compared to major search engines.

Verdict

Final Review

Ecosia is one of the easiest ways to add an environmental dimension to your daily digital routine. Simply switching your default search engine means your existing habits start working for the planet without any extra effort or cost. While search quality occasionally lags behind Google for complex queries, the tradeoff is negligible for most everyday browsing. For anyone who wants a simple, impactful, and privacy-respecting way to reduce their footprint, Ecosia is an outstanding choice that we wholeheartedly recommend.
