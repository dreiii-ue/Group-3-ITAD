Sustainable Web Browsing

Search the Web.
Plant Trees.

Ecosia is a search engine that uses its ad revenue to plant trees where nature and people need them most, allowing you to fight climate change just by surfing the web.

Ecosia

What is Ecosia?

The Eco-Friendly Search Engine

Ecosia works just like Google, but with a major difference: 100% of its profits go towards climate action, with the majority dedicated to planting trees around the world. It’s a simple, free way to make your daily digital habits environmentally sustainable.

How To Use

Make an Impact in 6 Steps

1

Get the App or Extension

Download Ecosia on iOS/Android or add the free extension to Chrome, Safari, or Edge.

2

Set as Default

Make Ecosia your default search engine so every query automatically contributes to the planet.

3

Search Normally

Browse the web, find answers, look up images, and read the news just like you normally would.

4

Watch Your Counter

Keep an eye on the personal tree counter in the top right corner to see your direct impact.

5

Learn About Projects

Click the tree icon to explore global reforestation projects funded by your searches.

6

Browse Privately

Search with peace of mind knowing Ecosia protects your privacy and doesn't sell your data.
