What is the Honor Band 7?

A Budget Band with a Premium Display

The Honor Band 7 is an affordable fitness tracker by Honor that punches well above its price with a stunning 1.47-inch full-color AMOLED display, 96 sport modes, continuous heart rate monitoring with high and low alerts, 24/7 SpO2 blood oxygen tracking, sleep quality analysis, stress monitoring with guided breathing, menstrual cycle tracking, and a battery that lasts up to 14 days, all paired with the Honor Health app for detailed health insights and device customization.

How To Use

Get Started with Honor Band 7 in 6 Simple Steps

1

Download the Honor Health App

Install the Honor Health app on your iOS or Android device. Create an Honor account or sign in to begin the setup process.

2

Pair via Bluetooth

Open the Honor Health app, tap the device icon, select "Add Device," choose Honor Band 7, and follow the Bluetooth pairing steps to connect to your phone.

3

Set Up Your Health Profile

Enter your personal information, age, height, weight, and fitness goals, so the Band 7 can calculate accurate health and activity data tailored to you.

4

Enable Health Monitoring

Turn on all-day heart rate monitoring, SpO2 tracking, stress monitoring, and sleep tracking in the app settings so the Band 7 records your health around the clock.

5

Start a Workout

Press the side button or swipe to the workout menu on the band, select from 96 available sport modes such as running, swimming, or cycling, and tap start to begin tracking.

6

Review Data in Honor Health

Sync your data to the Honor Health app after each session to view workout reports, sleep analysis, heart rate trends, stress levels, and SpO2 measurements.
