What is the Xiaomi Mi Band?

A Premium Fitness Band at a Budget Price

The Xiaomi Mi Band (Smart Band series) is one of the world's best-selling fitness trackers, known for packing premium health monitoring features into a lightweight, affordable wristband. It features a bright AMOLED display, continuous heart rate and SpO2 monitoring, detailed sleep tracking with REM analysis, 30+ sports modes, stress tracking, and up to 21 days of battery life, all paired with the Mi Fitness app for in-depth health insights and data analysis.

How To Use

Get Started with Xiaomi Mi Band in 6 Simple Steps

1

Download the Mi Fitness App

Install the Mi Fitness app (formerly Mi Fit / Zepp Life) on your iOS or Android phone. Create or sign in to your Xiaomi account to get started.

2

Pair the Band via Bluetooth

Enable Bluetooth on your phone, open Mi Fitness, tap "Add Device," and select your Mi Band model. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete pairing.

3

Set Up Your Health Profile

Enter your age, height, weight, and fitness goals in the Mi Fitness app so the band can accurately calculate calories, step goals, and health metrics.

4

Enable Health Monitoring Features

Turn on continuous heart rate monitoring, SpO2 tracking, sleep monitoring, and stress detection in the app settings for round-the-clock health awareness.

5

Start a Workout

Swipe to the workout menu on the band, select your sport mode, such as outdoor running, swimming, cycling, or HIIT, and tap to begin tracking your session.

6

Review Data in Mi Fitness

After syncing, open the Mi Fitness app to review detailed workout summaries, sleep reports, heart rate graphs, SpO2 trends, and weekly health insights.
