What is the Xiaomi Mi Band?

Premium Fitness Tracking Made Accessible

The Xiaomi Mi Band is a global bestseller that packs high-end sensors for sleep, heart rate, and blood oxygen into an affordable, water-resistant wearable.

Editor's Rating: 4.7 / 5

How To Use

Get Started in 6 Simple Steps

1

Download Mi Fitness

Install the app on iOS or Android and sign in to your Xiaomi account.

2

Pair Your Band

Enable Bluetooth, tap "Add Device" in the app, and select your Mi Band model.

3

Set Health Profile

Input your metrics and fitness goals for accurate calorie and step tracking.

4

Enable Monitoring

Activate 24/7 heart rate, SpO2, and sleep tracking in the app settings.

5

Start a Workout

Select from 30+ modes on your wrist and tap to begin your tracking session.

6

Review Insights

Sync to the app to view detailed health graphs and weekly trend reports.

Evaluation

Pros & Cons

Pros

  • Unbeatable battery life lasting up to 3 weeks on a single charge.
  • Wide range of strap options and digital watch faces.
  • Fast and accurate sensor data for daily activities.
  • Excellent ecosystem integration with the Mi Fitness app.

Cons

  • Small screen size may be difficult for some users to read.
  • No built-in GPS on base models (uses phone GPS).
  • Sleep tracking can occasionally record sedentary time as light sleep.
  • Automatic workout detection can be slow to trigger.

Verdict

Final Review

The Xiaomi Mi Band remains the undisputed king of budget fitness trackers. It perfectly balances price, functionality, and design. While serious athletes might miss built-in GPS, it is an ideal companion for the vast majority of users looking to improve their daily activity, monitor their sleep, and stay informed about their overall health without spending a fortune.
