The Samsung Galaxy Fit 2 is a lightweight, stylish fitness band with automatic workout detection, 24/7 heart rate monitoring, sleep tracking, 15-day battery life, and seamless Samsung Galaxy integration — managed through the Samsung Health app.

Samsung Galaxy Fit 2

What is the Samsung Galaxy Fit 2?

Samsung's Compact All-Day Activity Tracker

The Samsung Galaxy Fit 2 is a slim, comfortable fitness band designed to track your daily activity and wellness with ease. It features a 1.1-inch color AMOLED display and seamless health tracking experience, especially on Galaxy smartphones.

Editor's Rating: 4.3 / 5

How To Use

Get Started in 6 Simple Steps

1

Install Essential Apps

Download Galaxy Wearable and Samsung Health apps on your Android phone to begin.

2

Pair via Bluetooth

Open Galaxy Wearable, select Galaxy Fit 2, and follow the prompts to connect.

3

Health Profile

Enter your age, weight, and goals in Samsung Health for accurate calorie tracking.

4

Custom Notifications

Enable call/text alerts and choose which data appears on your band's display.

5

Auto-Detection

Wear it during physical activity; it automatically records walks, runs, and more.

6

Review Data

Open Samsung Health to view heart rate history, sleep patterns, and daily summaries.

Automatically recognizes 5 different workout types so you don't have to start them manually.

Sleep Management

Track sleep stages and receive a Sleep Score to help improve your nightly rest quality.

Water Resistance

IP68 and 5ATM water resistance allows for tracking during swims and in wet conditions.

15-Day Battery

Long-lasting power that keeps up with your habits without the need for daily charging.

Benefits

Why Users Choose Galaxy Fit 2

Evaluation

Pros & Cons

Pros

  • Vibrant and bright AMOLED display even in direct sunlight.
  • Excellent battery life for the price point.
  • Very thin and slim profile makes it ideal for sleep tracking.
  • Reliable notification syncing and music controls.

Cons

  • No built-in GPS (relies on phone's GPS).
  • Strap can be tricky to fasten for some users.
  • Lacks an Always-On Display feature to save battery.
  • Limited watch face customization options.

Verdict

Final Review

The Samsung Galaxy Fit 2 is a top-tier choice for budget-conscious fitness enthusiasts. While it doesn't offer the advanced GPS capabilities of high-end smartwatches, its 15-day battery life and seamless integration with the Samsung Health ecosystem make it an unbeatable companion for daily activity tracking. It is highly recommended for those who prioritize comfort, battery longevity, and essential health metrics over professional-grade athletic features.
