What is the Amazfit Band 7?

A Feature-Rich Fitness Band Under $50

The Amazfit Band 7 is a smartwatch-inspired fitness band by Amazfit that delivers exceptional value under $50. It features a bright 1.47-inch always-on AMOLED display, 112% larger than its predecessor, with 120 sports modes, Alexa built-in for voice commands, 24/7 heart rate and SpO2 monitoring, sleep and stress tracking, the Zepp PAI health score system, VO₂ Max analysis, 5ATM water resistance, and up to 18 days of battery life, all managed through the Zepp app.

How To Use

Get Started with Amazfit Band 7 in 6 Simple Steps

1

Download the Zepp App

Install the Zepp app on your iOS or Android phone and create a Zepp/Amazfit account. The app manages all your health data and band settings.

2

Pair the Band via Bluetooth

In the Zepp app, tap the profile icon, select "Add Device," choose Amazfit Band 7, and follow the Bluetooth pairing steps to link it to your phone.

3

Set Up Your Health Profile

Enter your age, height, weight, and fitness goal in the Zepp app so the Band 7 can calculate your PAI score and personalize your daily health targets.

4

Enable Health & Smart Features

Turn on 24-hour heart rate monitoring, SpO2 tracking, sleep monitoring, and stress detection in the Zepp app settings. Enable Alexa for voice command support.

5

Start a Workout

Swipe to the Workout menu on the band, choose from 120 sport modes, and tap Start. After finishing, view your post-workout data including VO₂ Max, recovery time, and training effect.

6

Track Your PAI Score & Progress

Check your Zepp PAI health score daily, a score of 100 or above indicates an active, heart-healthy lifestyle. Review all health and workout data in the Zepp app dashboard.
