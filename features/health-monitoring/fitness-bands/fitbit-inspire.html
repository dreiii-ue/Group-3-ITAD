What is Fitbit Inspire Series?

Fitbit's Beginner-Friendly Wellness Tracker

The Fitbit Inspire series is Fitbit's entry-level tracker designed for building healthier habits through slim AMOLED touchscreens and comprehensive health monitoring.

Editor's Rating: 4.4 / 5

How To Use

Get Started in 6 Simple Steps

1

Download Fitbit App

Install on iOS/Android and sign in with a Google account.

2

Pair Your Device

Charge fully and follow Bluetooth pairing instructions in the app.

3

Personal Profile

Input age, height, and weight for accurate calorie and step tracking.

4

Workout Shortcuts

Choose up to 6 modes like yoga or cycling for quick launch.

5

Automatic Tracking

Wear it day and night for steps, calories, and sleep stages.

6

Review Your Stats

Check Sleep Score, stress levels, and readiness in the dashboard.

Evaluation

Pros & Cons

Pros

  • Extremely lightweight and slim profile for 24/7 comfort.
  • Industry-leading sleep tracking and data analysis.
  • Impressive 10-day battery life significantly outperforms smartwatches.
  • User-friendly app interface that is easy for beginners.

Cons

  • Does not have built-in GPS (uses phone's GPS).
  • Full data access requires a monthly Fitbit Premium subscription.
  • Small screen can be difficult to read for some users.
  • Limited third-party app support compared to full smartwatches.

Verdict

Final Review

The Fitbit Inspire series remains the gold standard for entry-level fitness tracking. Its greatest strength lies in its simplicity—providing deep health insights without the bulk or complexity of a full smartwatch. While the reliance on a phone for GPS and the Premium subscription for full data might be drawbacks for power users, its battery life and superior sleep tracking make it the perfect choice for anyone looking to quietly improve their daily wellness.
