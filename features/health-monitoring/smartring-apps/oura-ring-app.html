Oura Ring helps users track sleep, activity, stress, recovery, and daily wellness through a compact smart ring and companion app. It provides round-the-clock insights that can help users build better habits and make smarter health decisions.

Oura Ring

What is Oura Ring?

A Smart Ring Focused on Sleep and Recovery

Oura Ring is a wearable health device that gives users detailed insights into sleep, readiness, activity, heart rate, and stress using continuous tracking throughout the day and night.

How To Use

Start Tracking in 6 Steps

1

Set Up Your Account

Create your Oura account and connect your ring to the mobile app.

2

Wear the Ring Daily

Keep it on during the day and while sleeping to collect health data.

3

Sync with the App

Open the app regularly to sync sleep, activity, and recovery metrics.

4

Review Your Scores

Check readiness, sleep, and activity insights to understand your condition.

5

Follow Recommendations

Use the app’s guidance to improve rest, recovery, and daily habits.

6

Track Long-Term Trends

Monitor progress over time and adjust your lifestyle based on the data.
