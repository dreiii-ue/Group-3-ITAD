Women-Centric Health Tracking

Track Your Vitals.
Designed for Her.

The Movano Evie Ring is a pioneering wearable designed specifically for women. It combines medical-grade sensors with elegant design to monitor sleep, activity, heart rate, and menstrual health without a monthly subscription.

Evie Ring

What is Evie Ring?

A Smarter Way to Understand Your Body

Evie Ring uses advanced biometrics and an open-design ring to provide women with personalized health insights into their daily cycles and recovery.

Editor's Rating: 4.6 / 5

How To Use

Start Tracking in 6 Steps

1

Size Your Finger

Use the Evie sizing kit to ensure a perfect fit for the open-design ring.

2

Download the App

Install the Evie App on your iOS device to sync data.

3

Sync via Bluetooth

Pair your ring effortlessly through the app interface.

4

Wear it Daily

Keep the ring on to track active minutes, steps, and heart rate.

5

Log Your Symptoms

Input daily moods and physical symptoms for hormonal insights.

6

Review Trends

Analyze your sleep stages and heart rate variability (HRV) patterns.

Evaluation

Pros & Cons

Pros

  • Beautiful, jewelry-like aesthetic that fits any style.
  • Unique open design prevents the ring from feeling too tight.
  • No hidden costs or ongoing membership requirements.
  • Deeply personalized women's health insights.

Cons

  • Android app is still in development.
  • Battery life is shorter than some competitors (approx. 4 days).
  • The charging case can be bulkier than expected.

Verdict

Final Review

The Movano Evie Ring is a breath of fresh air in the wearable market. By prioritizing female physiology and removing the barrier of monthly subscriptions, it offers a sustainable and stylish way to monitor health. While Android users must wait for compatibility, iOS users will find it to be a reliable, accurate, and incredibly comfortable tracker that feels more like jewelry than a piece of tech.
