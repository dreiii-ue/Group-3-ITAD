What is Movano Evie Ring?

A Smart Ring Designed for Women’s Health

Evie Ring is a wellness wearable created with women-focused design and tracking in mind, helping users monitor sleep, heart rate, blood oxygen, temperature trends, activity, and menstrual health in one app-based experience.

How To Use

Start Tracking in 6 Steps

1

Set Up the App

Download the Evie app and create your account.

2

Pair Your Ring

Connect the ring and complete the initial setup process.

3

Wear It Daily

Use it throughout the day and during sleep for better wellness tracking.

4

Check Health Metrics

Review heart rate, sleep, blood oxygen, and activity insights in the app.

5

Track Personal Goals

Follow your progress using custom wellness goals and health priorities.

6

Monitor Patterns Over Time

Use long-term trends to better understand your body and daily routines.
