Circular Ring is a smart ring platform designed for continuous health tracking, including sleep, stress, activity, and heart-related insights. It also promotes personalized recommendations through its companion app experience.

Circular Ring

What is Circular Ring?

A Smart Ring for Everyday Wellness Tracking

Circular Ring helps users monitor daily health and lifestyle signals such as sleep, activity, stress, and heart-related metrics while offering app-based recommendations for healthier routines.

How To Use

Start Tracking in 6 Steps

1

Install the App

Download the Circular app and create your account.

2

Pair Your Ring

Connect the ring to your device and complete the setup process.

3

Wear It Daily

Keep it on as you sleep, move, and go through your daily routine.

4

Review Your Metrics

Check sleep, activity, energy, and wellness insights in the app.

5

Follow Recommendations

Use the suggestions to improve routines, recovery, and overall wellness.

6

Track Long-Term Progress

Look at your trends over time to understand patterns in your health.
