Ultrahuman Ring AIR helps users monitor sleep, movement, and recovery in a lightweight smart ring form. It is designed for everyday wear and gives users access to wellness insights without requiring a recurring subscription for their data.

Ultrahuman Ring AIR

What is Ultrahuman Ring AIR?

A Lightweight Smart Ring for Daily Wellness

Ultrahuman Ring AIR is a compact wearable that focuses on sleep, movement, recovery, and lifestyle awareness through continuous tracking and a mobile app experience.

How To Use

Start Tracking in 6 Steps

1

Create Your Account

Sign in to the Ultrahuman app and pair your ring.

2

Wear the Ring Consistently

Use it throughout the day and night for more complete data.

3

Sync with the App

Open the app to review recorded sleep, activity, and recovery metrics.

4

Review Recovery Insights

Check daily summaries to understand how your body is responding.

5

Adjust Daily Habits

Use the data to improve rest, movement, and overall wellness.

6

Track Trends Over Time

Compare patterns in your sleep, activity, and recovery week by week.
