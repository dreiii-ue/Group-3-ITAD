What is Azumio?

The World's Most Accurate Heart Rate App

Instant Heart Rate is a top-rated cardiovascular tool used by millions. By placing your finger over the camera lens, the app detects tiny color changes in your skin to calculate your heart rate, helping you monitor stress levels and fitness recovery instantly.

Editor's Rating: 4.8 / 5

How To Use

Measure Your Pulse in 6 Steps

1

Download & Launch

Get Instant Heart Rate on the App Store or Google Play and open the app.

2

Position Your Finger

Lightly place the tip of your index finger on the phone's back camera lens.

3

Cover the Flash

Ensure your finger also covers the flash to provide the necessary light for detection.

4

Hold Still

Stay calm and remain still for about 10 seconds while the app processes your pulse.

5

View Real-Time Data

Watch your PPG graph appear as the app calculates your beats per minute (BPM).

6

Tag and Save

Add notes like "just woke up" or "post-workout" to track your heart health trends.

Evaluation

Pros & Cons

Pros

  • No extra hardware required; works on almost any smartphone with a camera.
  • Clinically proven algorithm provides accuracy comparable to pulse oximeters.
  • Easy-to-understand visualization of heart rate zones and recovery.

Cons

  • Requires a clean camera lens; smudges can lead to inaccurate readings.
  • Results can be affected by cold fingers or poor lighting conditions.
  • The free version includes ads and limits the number of saved measurements.

Verdict

Final Review

Instant Heart Rate by Azumio is the gold standard for mobile pulse monitoring. Its simplicity and high degree of accuracy make it an indispensable tool for anyone without a dedicated smartwatch who still wants to keep a close eye on their heart health. While it cannot replace professional medical equipment for chronic conditions, it is an excellent "check-in" tool for stress management and workout recovery tracking.
