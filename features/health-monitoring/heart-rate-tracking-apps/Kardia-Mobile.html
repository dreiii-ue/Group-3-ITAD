Check Your Heart Anywhere

Track Your Heart, Stay Safe.

KardiaMobile is a simple tool that checks your heart rhythm. It works with your phone to tell you if your heart is beating normally in just 30 seconds.

Kardia

What is KardiaMobile?

A Doctor-Trusted Heart Tracker

KardiaMobile is a small device that fits in your pocket. It connects to an app on your phone to record your heart’s electrical activity. It is much more powerful than a basic fitness tracker because it can spot irregular heartbeats that other apps might miss.


How To Use

Check Your Heart in 6 Easy Steps

1

Open the App

Open the Kardia app on your phone. Make sure your phone's volume is up so the device can connect.

2

Place Your Fingers

Put your phone on a table. Place two fingers from each hand on the silver pads of the Kardia device.

3

Stay Still and Quiet

Don't move or talk for 30 seconds. The app will start drawing a line that shows your heartbeat.

4

Say How You Feel

You can tell the app if you feel dizzy or if your heart is racing. This helps keep a better record.

5

Get Your Result

The app will immediately tell you if your heart rhythm looks normal or if it finds an issue.

6

Send to a Doctor

If you're worried, you can send the report to your doctor as an email right from the app.
