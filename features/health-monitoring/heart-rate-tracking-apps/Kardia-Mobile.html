KardiaMobile is a medical-grade personal EKG that empowers you to take a 30-second EKG anytime, anywhere. It provides instant detection of Atrial Fibrillation, Bradycardia, Tachycardia, and Normal heart rhythms with clinical accuracy.

What is KardiaMobile?

A Doctor-Trusted Heart Tracker

KardiaMobile is a pocket-sized device that records your heart’s electrical activity, providing a deeper look at your heart health than standard smartwatches.

Editor's Rating: 4.8 / 5

How To Use

Check Your Heart in 6 Steps

1

Open the App

Launch the Kardia app and ensure your phone's volume is audible for connection.

2

Place Your Fingers

Rest your fingers on the silver sensors to begin the electrical reading.

3

Stay Still

Remain quiet and still for 30 seconds while the device records your rhythm.

4

Note Your Symptoms

Tag how you feel (e.g., dizzy, racing heart) for a more complete medical record.

5

Review Analysis

Get an instant FDA-cleared analysis of your heart rhythm on your screen.

6

Share with Doctors

Email a PDF of your EKG report directly to your physician from the app.

Evaluation

Pros & Cons

Pros

  • Unmatched accuracy for a consumer device.
  • Extremely easy to use for all age groups.
  • Seamless integration with smartphone apps.
  • Highly recommended by cardiologists worldwide.

Cons

  • Requires a separate hardware purchase.
  • KardiaCare subscription needed for advanced reports.
  • Can be sensitive to electrical interference during use.
  • Does not provide continuous monitoring like a watch.

Verdict

Final Review

KardiaMobile is the gold standard for personal heart health monitoring. Its ability to provide clinical-grade EKG data in seconds makes it an invaluable tool for anyone managing heart conditions or seeking peace of mind. Highly recommended for its reliability and medical-grade precision.
