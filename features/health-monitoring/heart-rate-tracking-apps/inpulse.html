InPulse Heart Rate Monitor offers a streamlined, user-friendly way to measure and record your heart rate using only your smartphone camera, helping you stay on top of your cardiovascular health.

InPulse

What is InPulse?

Simple & Reliable Heart Monitoring

InPulse is a dedicated heart rate monitoring application designed for accuracy and ease of use. By leveraging advanced camera algorithms to track blood flow in your finger, it provides real-time BPM data to help users track resting heart rates, recovery times, and general fitness progress.


How To Use

Track Your BPM in 6 Steps

1

Open InPulse

Launch the InPulse app on your mobile device and navigate to the measurement screen.

2

Clean the Lens

Ensure your rear camera lens is clean to allow for the most accurate optical reading.

3

Cover the Camera

Gently place your index finger over the back camera so it completely covers the lens.

4

Stay Consistent

Apply light, steady pressure. Pressing too hard can restrict blood flow and affect the reading.

5

Wait for Analysis

Keep your finger in place for 10-15 seconds while the app analyzes your pulse wave.

6

Log Your Activity

Save the result and categorize it under labels like "Resting," "Exercise," or "Before Sleep."
