Your Pulse, Simplified.

InPulse Heart Rate Monitor offers a streamlined, user-friendly way to measure and record your heart rate using only your smartphone camera, helping you stay on top of your cardiovascular health.

InPulse

What is InPulse?

Simple & Reliable Heart Monitoring

InPulse is a dedicated heart rate monitoring application designed for accuracy and ease of use. By leveraging advanced camera algorithms to track blood flow in your finger, it provides real-time BPM data.

Editor's Rating: 4.8 / 5

How To Use

Track Your BPM in 6 Steps

1

Open InPulse

Launch the InPulse app and go to the measurement screen.

2

Clean the Lens

Ensure your rear camera lens is clean for accurate optical reading.

3

Cover the Camera

Place your index finger over the back camera lens completely.

4

Stay Consistent

Apply light, steady pressure to avoid restricting blood flow.

5

Wait for Analysis

Keep your finger in place for 10-15 seconds for analysis.

6

Log Your Activity

Save the result and categorize it with relevant labels.

Verdict

Final Review

InPulse is a highly recommended tool for anyone prioritizing cardiovascular awareness. Its ability to turn a standard smartphone into a health monitor makes it incredibly accessible. While it shouldn't replace medical-grade equipment for chronic conditions, it serves as a fantastic daily companion for tracking fitness progress and general wellbeing.
