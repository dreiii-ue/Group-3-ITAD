AI Health Analysis

Manage Stress, Optimize Energy.

Welltory uses Heart Rate Variability (HRV) to analyze your health, productivity, and stress levels, providing science-backed insights into your body's performance.

Welltory

What is Welltory?

Your AI-Powered Health Coach

Welltory is an advanced health data platform that syncs with over 1,000 apps and gadgets. By measuring your HRV via your phone's camera or a wearable, it provides a daily "body report" that shows how stressed you are and how much energy you have left for work or exercise.


How To Use

Optimize Your Day in 6 Steps

1

Download & Sync

Install Welltory and connect your favorite health apps (Apple Health, Google Fit, or Garmin).

2

Start a Measurement

Tap the liquid measurement button and place your finger on the camera lens and flash.

3

Relax Your Body

Keep perfectly still and breathe naturally for about two minutes while the AI analyzes your HRV.

4

Check Stress Scores

Review your Stress, Energy, and Health scores to see your current physiological state.

5

Read Your Insights

Scroll through personalized "stories" that explain how your sleep or activity affected your scores.

6

Follow Suggestions

Adjust your schedule based on the AI's advice—like taking a break or pushing through a workout.
