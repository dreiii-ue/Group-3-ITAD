Manage Stress,

Welltory uses Heart Rate Variability (HRV) to analyze your health, productivity, and stress levels, providing science-backed insights into your body's performance. It helps you understand how your lifestyle impacts your physiology in real-time.

Welltory

What is Welltory?

Your AI-Powered Health Coach

Welltory is an advanced health data platform that syncs with over 1,000 apps and gadgets. By measuring your HRV via your phone's camera or a wearable, it provides a daily "body report" that shows how stressed you are and how much energy you have left for work or exercise.

Editor's Rating: 4.5 / 5

How To Use

Optimize Your Day in 6 Steps

1

Download & Sync

Install Welltory and connect your favorite health apps like Apple Health or Google Fit.

2

Start a Measurement

Tap the measurement button and place your finger on the camera lens and flash.

3

Relax Your Body

Keep perfectly still and breathe naturally for about two minutes for accurate HRV analysis.

4

Check Stress Scores

Review your Stress, Energy, and Health scores to see your current physiological state.

5

Read Your Insights

Scroll through personalized "stories" that explain how lifestyle factors affect your scores.

6

Follow Suggestions

Adjust your schedule based on the AI's advice, such as taking a break or training harder.

Evaluation

Pros & Cons

Pros

  • Unmatched breadth of third-party app and wearable integrations.
  • Detailed scientific insights into autonomic nervous system balance.
  • Highly personalized feedback that adapts to your unique baseline.

Cons

  • Many advanced analytics and trends require a premium subscription.
  • The interface can feel overwhelming due to the sheer volume of data.
  • Camera-based measurements require very steady hands for accuracy.

Verdict

Final Review

Welltory is an exceptional tool for those who want a deep, data-driven understanding of their body. While standard trackers tell you how many steps you took, Welltory tells you how those steps—and your stress—actually affected your internal recovery. It is a powerful preventive tool for managing burnout and optimizing daily performance, making it highly recommended for both athletes and high-performance professionals.
