Wireless Oxygen Tracking

Simple. Smart.
FDA Approved.

The iHealth Air is a simple, smart pulse oximeter that connects directly to your smartphone to log your SpO2 and pulse rate. It offers clinically tested accuracy and a seamless digital recording of your respiratory vitals.

iHealth Air

What is iHealth Air?

Effortless Health Logging

By syncing seamlessly with the iHealth MyVitals app, this device removes the hassle of manual health journals and gives you an instant digital record of your respiratory vitals.

Editor's Rating: 4.4 / 5

How To Use

Log Your Vitals in 6 Steps

1

Get MyVitals App

Download the companion app to act as your digital health journal.

2

Connect Device

Pair the pulse oximeter to your smartphone via Bluetooth.

3

Take Measurement

Rest your hand, insert your finger, and press the single button.

4

Read Display

View the immediate results on the device's LED screen.

5

Sync Automatically

Monitor Health

Evaluation

Pros & Cons

Pros

  • High medical-grade accuracy for home use.
  • Elegant, minimalist hardware design with clear LED.
  • Fast and reliable Bluetooth connectivity.
  • Rechargeable battery lasts for hundreds of readings.

Cons

  • Requires the app for long-term data storage.
  • Hardware is more expensive than non-smart alternatives.
  • The MyVitals app can occasionally feel cluttered.
  • No built-in storage on the device without a phone sync.

Verdict

Final Review

iHealth Air is a top-tier choice for digital health tracking. Its combination of FDA-cleared precision and seamless smartphone integration makes it a standout tool for anyone monitoring respiratory conditions or athletic recovery. While it carries a premium price point, the ease of maintaining a digital health history and sharing reports with doctors justifies the investment. Highly recommended for users seeking a modern, reliable pulse oximeter.
