Continuous Overnight Tracking

Sleep Safer.
Monitor Constantly.

The Wellue O2Ring is a comfortable, wearable ring that continuously tracks your blood oxygen levels and heart rate overnight. It features medical-grade sensors and a built-in vibration alarm to alert you to low oxygen levels or abnormal heart rates.

Wellue O2Ring

What is Wellue O2Ring?

Your Silent Guardian

Designed specifically for overnight monitoring, the O2Ring vibrates gently to wake you if your oxygen levels drop or your heart rate becomes abnormal.

Editor's Rating: 4.8 / 5

How To Use

Track Overnight in 6 Steps

1

Charge the Ring

Ensure the device is fully charged before going to bed.

2

Wear on Finger

Slip the comfortable silicone ring onto your thumb or index finger.

3

Go to Sleep

The ring automatically turns on and begins recording your biometrics continuously.

4

Sync Your Data

Open the ViHealth app in the morning to transfer the night's data via Bluetooth.

5

Review Reports

Check the high-resolution charts for any oxygen drops during the night.

6

Customize Alerts

Adjust the vibration threshold for low oxygen or abnormal heart rates in the app settings.

Evaluation

Pros & Cons

Pros

  • Unmatched comfort compared to traditional fingertip oximeters.
  • Real-time vibration alerts prevent prolonged oxygen desaturation.
  • Detailed data resolution (every 4 seconds) for accurate sleep studies.
  • Long battery life suitable for multi-night use.

Cons

  • Higher price point than basic pulse oximeters.
  • Requires daily syncing to clear device memory for long sessions.
  • The app interface can be overwhelming for non-technical users.
  • Proprietary charging cable required.

Verdict

Final Review

The Wellue O2Ring is a top-tier health monitoring tool for anyone concerned about sleep quality or respiratory health. Its combination of a non-intrusive form factor and reliable medical-grade data makes it the gold standard for home sleep apnea monitoring. While it is an investment, the peace of mind provided by its vibration alert system and the high-quality data available for medical professionals makes it a highly recommended tool for long-term health management.
