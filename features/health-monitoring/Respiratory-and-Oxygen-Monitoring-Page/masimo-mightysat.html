Medical-Grade Oximetry

Hospital Tech at
Your Fingertips.

The Masimo MightySat provides industry-leading accuracy in a fingertip pulse oximeter, measuring oxygen saturation, pulse rate, and hydration levels.

Masimo MightySat

What is Masimo MightySat?

Premium Respiratory Tracking

Masimo utilizes Signal Extraction Technology (SET) to provide highly accurate readings even during motion and low blood flow, making it ideal for athletes and health tracking.

How To Use

Monitor Oxygen in 6 Steps

1

Download the App

Install the Masimo Personal Health app on your smartphone.

2

Turn on Bluetooth

Ensure your phone's Bluetooth is active to sync the device.

3

Insert Finger

Place your finger comfortably into the device until the screen lights up.

4

View Instant Readings

Read your SpO2, Pulse Rate, and Perfusion Index directly on the screen.

5

Track Your Trends

Open the app to see historical data and chart your respiratory health.

6

Share with Doctors

Export your data easily to share with healthcare professionals.
