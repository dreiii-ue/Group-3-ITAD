What is Masimo MightySat?

Premium Respiratory Tracking

Masimo utilizes Signal Extraction Technology (SET) to provide highly accurate readings even during motion and low blood flow, making it ideal for athletes and health tracking.

Evaluation

Pros & Cons

Pros

  • Industry-leading accuracy with Signal Extraction Technology.
  • Measures unique metrics like Perfusion Index and PVi.
  • Durable, hospital-grade build quality.
  • Reliable Bluetooth connectivity with the Masimo Health app.

Cons

  • Higher price point compared to consumer-grade oximeters.
  • Advanced metrics like PVi may require a learning curve to interpret.
  • Requires specific placement for the most accurate readings.
  • The app interface can feel clinical rather than fitness-focused.

Verdict

Final Review

Masimo MightySat is the gold standard for personal respiratory monitoring. While it carries a premium price, the reliability of its hospital-grade sensors makes it an essential tool for those managing chronic conditions or athletes training at peak levels. Its ability to provide accurate data during movement sets it apart from almost every other fingertip oximeter on the market.
