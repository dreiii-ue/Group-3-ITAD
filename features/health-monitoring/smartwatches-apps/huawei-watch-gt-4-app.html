What is Huawei Watch GT 4?

Evaluation

Pros & Cons

Pros

  • Stunning, high-end design that looks like a traditional luxury watch.
  • Incredible battery life that significantly outlasts Apple and Samsung.
  • Very competitive pricing for the build quality and materials used.
  • Excellent fitness and sleep tracking accuracy via TruSeen 5.5+.

Cons

  • Lack of a massive third-party app ecosystem compared to WearOS.
  • Limited smart features (no cellular/LTE model available).
  • Huawei Health app installation can be tricky on some Android phones.
  • NFC payments are restricted in many Western regions.

Verdict

Final Review

The Huawei Watch GT 4 is the ultimate choice for users who prioritize battery life and aesthetics. It functions more like a professional-grade fitness tracker disguised as a designer watch. While it may lack the complex "app-first" approach of the Apple Watch, its longevity and superior health sensors make it a formidable companion for active lifestyles.
