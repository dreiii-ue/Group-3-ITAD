What is Huawei Watch GT 4?

The Master of Endurance and Health

The Huawei Watch GT 4 is a premium smartwatch designed for those who refuse to compromise on style or performance. Featuring TruSeen™ 5.5+ technology, it provides precise heart rate readings even during intense workouts.
