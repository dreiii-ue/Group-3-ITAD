The Galaxy Wearable app connects your Galaxy Watch, Buds, and Fits to your mobile device, allowing you to manage and monitor wearable features and apps with ease.

Galaxy Wearable

What is Galaxy Wearable?

The Gateway to Your Smart Ecosystem

The Galaxy Wearable application is the command center for your Samsung devices. It allows you to customize watch faces, configure settings, and update software for your Galaxy Watch and Galaxy Buds.

Setup Guide

Master Your Wearable in 6 Easy Steps

1

Launch the App

Open Galaxy Wearable on your phone and ensure Bluetooth is enabled.

2

Connect Device

Select your Galaxy Watch or Buds from the list to start pairing.

3

Customize Layouts

Arrange your apps and widgets exactly how you want them on your watch.

4

Configure Alerts

Choose which smartphone notifications should appear on your wearable.

5

Sync Health Data

Enable Samsung Health integration to track workouts, sleep, and stress.

6

Update Firmware

Check for the latest software updates to keep your devices running smoothly.
