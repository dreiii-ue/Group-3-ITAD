What is Fitbit Versa?

A Versatile Health & Fitness Smartwatch

Fitbit Versa provides a holistic view of your health by monitoring your physical activity, sleep quality, and stress levels. It combines smart features with deep health metrics to keep you energized throughout the day.

How To Use

Master Your Wellness in 6 Steps

1

Pair with Fitbit App

Download the Fitbit app on your smartphone and sync your Versa via Bluetooth to begin.

2

Customize Your Dashboard

Choose the health metrics you want to see first, such as steps, calories, or heart rate.

3

Track Your Workouts

Use 20+ goal-based exercise modes or let SmartTrack automatically record your activity.

4

Check Active Zone Minutes

Feel a buzz when you reach your target heart rate zones during exercise.

5

Monitor Sleep & Stress

Receive a daily Sleep Score and use the EDA Scan sensor to manage your stress levels.

6

Sync & Progress

Review your weekly trends and health data summaries within the Fitbit mobile app.
