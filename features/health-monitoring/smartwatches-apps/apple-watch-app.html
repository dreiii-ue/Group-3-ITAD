What is Apple Watch?

A Revolutionary Fitness Companion

Apple Watch is more than just a wearable; it's a dedicated fitnesss coach on your wrist. By using advanced sensors, it monitors everything from your heart rate to your blood oxygen levels, and even your sleep patterns through its iconic "Rings" system.

How To Use

Master Your Health in 6 Easy Steps

1

Pair with iPhone

Open the Watch app on your iPhone to sync your health data and settings.

2

Set Activity Goals

Customize your Move, Exercise, and Stand goals based on your personal fitness level.

3

Start a Workout

Use the Workout app to track specific activities like Running, Cycling, or Yoga.

4

Monitor Heart Health

Set up notifications for high/low heart rates and check your ECG anytime.

5

Track Your Sleep

Wear your watch to bed to see how much time you spend in REM or Deep sleep.

6

Review Trends

Check the Fitness app on your iPhone to see long-term progress and awards.
