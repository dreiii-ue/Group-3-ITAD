What is Sleep Cycle?

Your Intelligent Sleep Companion

Sleep Cycle is an award-winning sleep tracker and smart alarm app. It uses sound analysis to detect your sleep movements throughout the night, maps your sleep stages, and wakes you at the optimal moment within a 30-minute window.


How To Use

Better Sleep in 6 Steps

1

Download the App

Install Sleep Cycle free on iOS or Android and create your account.

2

Set Alarm Window

Choose a 30-minute wake-up window (e.g., 6:30–7:00 AM).

3

Place Your Phone

Set the phone face-down on your mattress or nightstand nearby.

4

Let It Listen

Sleep Cycle uses your microphone to detect breathing and movement.

5

Wake Up Gently

The alarm triggers during light sleep for a natural awakening.

6

Review Your Sleep

Explore nightly sleep graphs, trends, and personalized tips.
