What is Sleep Cycle?

Your Intelligent Sleep Companion

Sleep Cycle is an award-winning sleep tracker that uses sound analysis to detect sleep movements, map stages, and wake you at the optimal moment.

Editor's Rating: 4.8 / 5

How To Use

Better Sleep in 6 Steps

1

Download the App

Install Sleep Cycle free on iOS or Android and create your account.

2

Set Alarm Window

Choose a 30-minute wake-up window (e.g., 6:30–7:00 AM).

3

Place Your Phone

Set the phone face-down on your mattress or nightstand nearby.

4

Let It Listen

The app uses your microphone to detect breathing and movement.

5

Wake Up Gently

The alarm triggers during light sleep for a natural awakening.

6

Review Your Sleep

Explore nightly sleep graphs, trends, and personalized tips.

Evaluation

Pros & Cons

Pros

  • No wearable device required; works via phone microphone.
  • Highly effective smart alarm minimizes morning grogginess.
  • Comprehensive data visualization for long-term trends.
  • Includes integrated tools for falling asleep (sounds/stories).

Cons

  • Requires phone to be placed close to the bed, which can be a distraction.
  • Premium features like long-term trends require a subscription.
  • Microphone tracking can be affected by pets or partners in the room.
  • Significant battery drain if the phone isn't plugged in overnight.

Verdict

Final Review

Sleep Cycle is a game-changer for anyone struggling with morning fatigue. Its ability to pinpoint the exact moment you transition into light sleep makes waking up feel significantly more natural. While the microphone-based tracking requires specific phone placement, the wealth of data and the quality of sleep aids make it one of the most reliable wellness apps available today.
