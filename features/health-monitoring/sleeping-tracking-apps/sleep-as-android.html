What is Sleep as Android?

Power Features for Serious Sleepers

Consistently ranked as the best sleep tracker for Android, this app offers more than just graphs. It provides ultra-accurate movement detection, snore recording with anti-snoring vibrations, and deep integration with Wear OS, Fitbit, Garmin, and Samsung wearables.
