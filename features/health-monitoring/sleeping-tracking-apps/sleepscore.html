What is SleepScore?

The Non-Contact Sleep Revolution

Powered by ResMed technology, SleepScore is the world's most advanced sleep app. It doesn't need a wearable; instead, it emits silent sound waves (sonar) that bounce off your body to measure every breath and movement. This data is then used to provide a "SleepScore" out of 100, validated against clinical sleep studies.
