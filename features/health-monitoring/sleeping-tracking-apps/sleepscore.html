What is SleepScore?

The Non-Contact Sleep Revolution

Powered by ResMed technology, SleepScore is the world's most advanced sleep app. It doesn't need a wearable; instead, it emits silent sound waves (sonar) that bounce off your body to measure every breath and movement.

Evaluation

Pros & Cons

Pros

  • No hardware or wearables required for full analysis.
  • Highly accurate detection of light, deep, and REM cycles.
  • Smart alarm is excellent at reducing "morning grogginess."
  • Excellent coaching programs for insomnia and fatigue.

Cons

  • Requires phone to be plugged in overnight.
  • Phone placement must be precise for sonar to work.
  • Sonar can be confused by fans or heavy white noise.
  • Advanced features require a monthly subscription.

Verdict

Final Review

SleepScore is a game-changer for those who find wearables intrusive or uncomfortable. Its ability to provide clinical-grade data through simple sonar technology is nothing short of impressive. While you do need to be careful with phone placement, the quality of the insights and the effectiveness of the smart alarm make it one of the best sleep tools on the market. It is a must-try for anyone looking to optimize their recovery without wearing a device.
