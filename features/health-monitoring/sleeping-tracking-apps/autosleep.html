AutoSleep automatically tracks your sleep from your Apple Watch. No buttons to press. No apps to start. Just wear your watch to bed and wake up to a complete analysis of your night.

AutoSleep

What is AutoSleep?

Silent, Precise, and Fully Automatic

AutoSleep is the gold standard for Apple Watch users who want zero-friction tracking. Using an advanced heuristic algorithm, it determines how long you've slept by analyzing your movement and heart rate. It even works if you don't wear your watch to bed by tracking when you last touched your phone.


How To Use

Better Data in 6 Steps

1

Initial Setup

Install AutoSleep on your iPhone and give permissions for HealthKit data.

2

Wear Your Watch

Simply wear your Apple Watch to bed. There is no "Start" button to press.

3

Wake Up Normally

The app detects you are awake when you start moving or check your phone.

4

Check Your Rings

View your Sleep, Quality, Deep Sleep, and Heart Rate rings in the morning.

5

Review Readiness

Check your "Readiness" score to see if your body is recovered for the day.

6

Tweak Sensitivity

Use the simple calibration wizard if you feel the app is too sensitive.
