Pillow is an all-in-one sleep assistant that uses your Apple Watch to analyze sleep cycles, heart rate, and even records important audio events during the night.

Pillow

What is Pillow?

The Advanced Sleep Lab in Your Pocket

Pillow can track your sleep automatically using your Apple Watch, or manually using your iPhone. It provides a comprehensive breakdown of your sleep stages (Deep, REM, Light) and integrates directly with Apple Health to give you a full picture of your cardiovascular health while you rest.


How To Use

Master Your Sleep in 6 Steps

1

Sync with Apple Watch

Download Pillow on your iPhone and Apple Watch for seamless tracking.

2

Enable Auto-Mode

Simply wear your watch to bed; Pillow detects when you fall asleep.

3

Record Audio Events

Optional: Enable microphone to record snoring, sleep talking, or apnea signs.

4

Heart Rate Analysis

Pillow overlays your heart rate data on your sleep stages for deeper insight.

5

Personalized Insights

Receive a morning briefing on how your habits affect your sleep quality.

6

Health App Sync

Automatically export sleep data to the Apple Health app for long-term tracking.
