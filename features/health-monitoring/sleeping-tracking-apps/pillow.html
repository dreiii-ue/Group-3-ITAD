Pillow is an all-in-one sleep assistant that uses your Apple Watch to analyze sleep cycles, heart rate, and even records important audio events during the night.

Pillow

What is Pillow?

The Advanced Sleep Lab in Your Pocket

Pillow can track your sleep automatically using your Apple Watch, or manually using your iPhone. It provides a comprehensive breakdown of your sleep stages (Deep, REM, Light) and integrates directly with Apple Health.

Editor's Rating: 4.8 / 5

How To Use

Master Your Sleep in 6 Steps

1

Sync with Apple Watch

Download Pillow on your iPhone and Apple Watch for seamless tracking.

2

Enable Auto-Mode

Simply wear your watch to bed; Pillow detects when you fall asleep.

3

Record Audio Events

Enable microphone to record snoring, sleep talking, or apnea signs.

4

Heart Rate Analysis

Pillow overlays your heart rate data on your sleep stages for deeper insight.

5

Personalized Insights

Receive a morning briefing on how your habits affect your sleep quality.

6

Health App Sync

Automatically export sleep data to the Apple Health app.

Why Apple Users Prefer Pillow

Evaluation

Pros & Cons

Pros

  • Beautifully designed user interface with easy-to-read charts.
  • Highly accurate automatic sleep detection with Apple Watch.
  • Deep integration with the Apple Health ecosystem.
  • Includes sound recording features for holistic sleep health.

Cons

  • Premium features require a subscription (Pillow Premium).
  • Limited functionality for users without an Apple Watch.
  • Can be battery-intensive if tracking manually on iPhone.
  • Occasional calibration issues with smart alarm timing.

Verdict

Final Review

Pillow is an outstanding sleep laboratory for your wrist. Its ability to seamlessly blend heart rate data with sleep stages provides a level of insight that few competitors can match. While the subscription cost might be a deterrent for some, the quality of information and the user-friendly interface make it an essential tool for anyone serious about improving their sleep hygiene and overall wellness.
