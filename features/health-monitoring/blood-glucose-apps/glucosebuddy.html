Glucose Buddy

What is Glucose Buddy?

The #1 Diabetes Tracking Tool

Glucose Buddy automates the tracking of blood sugar, carb intake, and physical activity by syncing seamlessly with Dexcom CGMs and Apple Health.

Editor's Rating: 4.8 / 5

How To Use

Start Tracking in 6 Steps

1

Download & Register

Install Glucose Buddy from the App Store or Google Play and create your secure profile.

2

Log Your Glucose

Enter your blood sugar readings manually or sync with compatible glucose meters via Bluetooth.

3

Track Meals & Carbs

Use the built-in food database to log your carbohydrate intake.

4

Record Medication

Set reminders and log your insulin doses or oral medications.

5

Monitor Activity

Integrate with Apple Health or Google Fit to track steps and exercise.

6

Export Reports

Generate detailed PDF reports of your logs to share with your doctor.

Evaluation

Pros & Cons

Pros

  • Extremely user-friendly interface for logging multi-factor data.
  • Robust integration with major health platforms like Apple Health.
  • "Meal IQ" provides unique insights into food triggers.
  • Highly professional reporting system for doctor visits.

Cons

  • Premium features require a subscription plan.
  • The food database can sometimes be overwhelming for new users.
  • Ad-supported in the free version.
  • Requires manual entry for non-Bluetooth meters.

Verdict

Final Review

Glucose Buddy remains the gold standard for comprehensive diabetes management. Its ability to synthesize blood sugar, diet, and exercise into a single cohesive story makes it an invaluable tool for anyone living with diabetes. While the premium subscription is an added cost, the depth of the "Meal IQ" insights and the quality of clinical reports make it a worthwhile investment for long-term metabolic health. Highly recommended for its reliability and professional data handling.
