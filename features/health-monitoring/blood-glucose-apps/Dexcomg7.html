Continuous Glucose Monitoring

Track Smarter.
Live with Confidence.

Dexcom G7 helps users monitor their glucose levels in real-time through a discreet wearable sensor. It offers personalized alerts, data-driven insights, and integrated sharing to help you manage your metabolic health and diabetes efficiently.

Dexcom G7

What is Dexcom G7?

A Data-Driven Health Companion

Dexcom G7 uses advanced sensor technology to provide continuous glucose readings that focus on accuracy and effortless management.

How To Use

Start Monitoring in 6 Steps

1

Apply the Sensor

Use the simple applicator to place the small sensor on the back of your upper arm.

2

Pair with the App

Open the G7 app and follow the prompts to pair your sensor via Bluetooth.

3

Complete Warm-up

Wait just 30 minutes for the sensor to calibrate and start sending data.

4

Set Custom Alerts

Configure high and low glucose notifications to suit your personal health goals.

5

Monitor Your Trends

Watch your glucose patterns and time-in-range metrics directly on your phone.

6

Share Your Data

Use the "Follow" feature to share real-time results with your doctor or loved ones.
