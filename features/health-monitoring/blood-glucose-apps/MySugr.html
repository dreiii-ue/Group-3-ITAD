App Capabilities

Core Features

Bolus Calculator

Clinically approved calculator that takes the guesswork out of insulin dosing based on carbs and current glucose.

Medical Reports

Generate PDF or CSV reports to share with your doctor during checkups for better treatment adjustments.

Multi-Device Sync

Sync with Apple Health, Google Fit, and Accu-Chek meters to keep all your health data in one place.

Smart Analysis

Visual trends and estimated HbA1c help you see the big picture of your diabetes control.

Evaluation

Pros & Cons

Pros

  • Highly engaging interface that reduces "log-out" burnout.
  • Seamless integration with Accu-Chek hardware.
  • Excellent PDF reporting for medical consultations.
  • Reliable estimated HbA1c tracking.

Cons

  • Some features like PDF reports require a Pro subscription.
  • Manual entry can still be time-consuming for non-synced meters.
  • Bolus calculator setup requires medical professional input.
  • App performance can vary on older smartphone models.

Verdict

Final Review

mySugr is a game-changer for anyone living with diabetes. By transforming the clinical chore of logging into a "monster-taming" game, it successfully bridges the gap between patient compliance and daily lifestyle. While the Pro features carry a cost, the ability to have a pocket mathematician calculate your insulin doses and provide instant A1c estimates makes it an indispensable tool for modern diabetes management.
