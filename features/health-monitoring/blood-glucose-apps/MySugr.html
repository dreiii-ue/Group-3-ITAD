mySugr is the loyal diabetes companion that makes daily logging fun and rewarding. By tracking your blood sugar, meals, and meds in one playful interface, it helps you "tame" your diabetes monster while providing clinical-grade insights and reports.

mySugr

What is mySugr?

The Pocket Mathematician for Health

The mySugr app features a "diabetes monster" that users can tame by logging data, turning the routine task of blood sugar tracking into an interactive and motivating game. It also integrates a bolus calculator and estimated HbA1c tracking to simplify insulin dosing and provide a real-time overview of long-term glycemic control.

How To Use

Manage Your Day in 6 Steps

1

Set Your Goals

Configure your target ranges and insulin-to-carb ratios during initial setup.

2

Sync Your Meter

Connect an Accu-Chek meter to automatically import glucose readings via Bluetooth.

3

Log Your Meals

Take a photo of your food and use tags like #pizza or #workout to track lifestyle impacts.

4

Calculate Bolus

Use the built-in Bolus Calculator to get precise insulin dose recommendations for mealtimes.

5

Check Your eA1c

Log at least 3 values a day for 7 days to see an estimated A1c right on your dashboard.

6

Tame the Monster

Complete daily challenges and earn points to keep your personal diabetes monster happy.
