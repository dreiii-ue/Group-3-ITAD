Peer Support Community

You Are Not Alone.
Find Your Community.

Bezzy T2D is a free online platform and app designed to connect people living with type 2 diabetes. Through shared stories, live discussions, and expert-reviewed content, Bezzy helps you navigate the daily challenges of diabetes with the support of people who truly understand.

Bezzy T2D

What is Bezzy T2D?

More Than Just a Health Tracker

Bezzy T2D focuses on the human side of health, offering a safe space for meaningful connections, emotional support, and practical advice from a global community of peers.

How To Use

Join the Community in 6 Steps

1

Sign Up for Free

Create your account on the Bezzy T2D website or download the mobile app to get started.

2

Build Your Profile

Share as much or as little as you'd like about your journey to find relevant community groups.

3

Explore Forums

Browse through various discussion categories like "Daily Life," "Food & Nutrition," or "Mental Health."

4

Join Live Hangouts

Participate in scheduled live video chats and discussions moderated by community leaders.

5

Connect One-on-One

Find and message members who have similar experiences or interests to build personal support links.

6

Stay Informed

Access a library of health articles and personal stories that provide tips for thriving with T2D.
