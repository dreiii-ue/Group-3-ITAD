Continuous Glucose Monitoring

The World's Smallest.
The Smartest.

FreeStyle Libre 3 is the smallest, thinnest glucose sensor ever designed. It automatically sends real-time glucose readings every minute to your smartphone, providing unsurpassed accuracy for up to 14 days of effortless health management.

What is FreeStyle Libre 3?

A Seamless Health Revolution

The FreeStyle Libre 3 system removes the need for routine fingersticks and manual scanning, providing a "zero-touch" experience that fits perfectly into an active lifestyle.

How To Use

Monitor Your Levels in 6 Steps

1

Apply the Sensor

Use the one-piece applicator to apply the tiny sensor to the back of your upper arm.

2

Initialize with App

Open the FreeStyle Libre 3 app on your smartphone and scan the sensor to start the connection.

3

Wait for Warm-up

Allow 60 minutes for the sensor to stabilize before it begins transmitting live data.

4

Configure Alarms

Set up optional glucose alarms to notify you immediately of low or high readings.

5

View Real-Time Data

Check your current glucose and trend arrows at a glance without ever having to scan the sensor.

6

Log Your Progress

Use the app to track food, exercise, and insulin to see how your body reacts in real-time.
