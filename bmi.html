What is BMI?

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a simple numerical measurement of your body weight in relation to your height. It provides a quick screening category to help identify potential weight issues.

At TechFit, we use this as a baseline metric. While it doesn't measure body fat directly, it serves as a valuable starting point for students and professionals to monitor their long-term health trends.

BMI Range Classification
Below 18.5 Underweight
18.5 – 24.9 Healthy Weight
25.0 – 29.9 Overweight
30.0 and Above Obese
