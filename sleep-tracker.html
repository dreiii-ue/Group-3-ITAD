The 90-Minute Rule

Understanding Sleep Cycles

Sleep is not a flat state of rest; it moves in cycles of approximately 90 minutes. Waking up in the middle of a cycle often results in "sleep inertia," leaving you feeling tired regardless of duration.

By timing your wake-up call to coincide with the end of a cycle, you can wake up feeling refreshed and mentally sharp for your daily tasks.

Sleep Hygiene

Sustainable Rest Tips

  • Digital Detox: Avoid blue light from screens 30 mins before bed.
  • Consistency: Try to sleep and wake at the same time every day.
  • Environment: Keep your room cool, dark, and quiet for deeper REM sleep.
